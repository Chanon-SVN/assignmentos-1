==Events==
* [[491]] &amp;ndash; Flavius Anastasius becomes [[Byzantine Emperor]], with the name of [[Anastasius I (emperor)|Anastasius I]].
*[[1079]] &amp;ndash; Bishop [[Stanislaus of Szczepanów|Stanislaus of Kraków]] is executed by order of [[Bolesław II the Generous|Bolesław II of Poland]].
*[[1241]] &amp;ndash; [[Batu Khan]] defeats [[Béla IV of Hungary]] at the [[Battle of Muhi]].
*[[1512]] &amp;ndash; [[War of the League of Cambrai]]: [[France|French]] forces led by [[Gaston of Foix, Duke of Nemours|Gaston de Foix]] win the [[Battle of Ravenna (1512)|Battle of Ravenna]].
*[[1544]] &amp;ndash; French forces defeat a Spanish army at the [[Battle of Ceresole]].
*[[1689]] &amp;ndash; [[William III of England|William III]] and [[Mary II of England|Mary II]] are crowned as joint sovereigns of [[Great Britain|Britain]].
*[[1713]] &amp;ndash; [[War of the Spanish Succession]] ([[Queen Anne's War]]): [[Treaty of Utrecht (1713)|Treaty of Utrecht]].
*[[1727]] &amp;ndash; Premiere of [[Johann Sebastian Bach]]'s [[St Matthew Passion]] BWV 244b at the [[St. Thomas Church, Leipzig]]
*[[1809]] &amp;ndash; [[Battle of the Basque Roads]] Naval battle fought between France and the [[United Kingdom]]
*[[1814]] &amp;ndash; The [[Treaty of Fontainebleau (1814)|Treaty of Fontainebleau]] ends the [[War of the Sixth Coalition]] against [[Napoleon Bonaparte]], and forces him to abdicate unconditionally for the first time.
*[[1856]] &amp;ndash; [[Second Battle of Rivas|Battle of Rivas]]: [[Juan Santamaria]] burns down the hostel where [[William Walker (filibuster)|William Walker]]'s [[filibuster (military)|filibusters]] are holed up.
*[[1868]] &amp;ndash; Former [[Shogun]] [[Tokugawa Yoshinobu]] surrenders [[Edo Castle]] to Imperial forces, marking the end of the [[Tokugawa shogunate]].
*[[1876]] &amp;ndash; The [[Benevolent and Protective Order of Elks]] is organized.
*[[1881]] &amp;ndash; [[Spelman College]] is founded in [[Atlanta|Atlanta, Georgia]] as the Atlanta Baptist Female Seminary, an institute of higher education for African-American women.
*[[1888]] &amp;ndash; The [[Concertgebouw]] in [[Amsterdam]] is inaugurated.
*[[1908]] &amp;ndash; {{SMS|Blücher}}, the last [[armored cruiser]] to be built by the [[German Imperial Navy]], launches.
*[[1909]] &amp;ndash; The city of [[Tel Aviv]] is founded.
*[[1913]] &amp;ndash; The [[Nevill Ground]]'s pavilion is destroyed in a [[suffragette]] arson attack becoming the only [[cricket]] ground to be attacked by suffragettes.
*[[1919]] &amp;ndash; The [[International Labour Organization]] is founded.
*[[1921]] &amp;ndash; [[Emir]] [[Abdullah I of Jordan|Abdullah]] establishes the first centralised government in the newly created [[British protectorate]] of [[Emirate of Transjordan|Transjordan]].
*[[1945]] &amp;ndash; [[World War II]]: American forces liberate the [[Buchenwald concentration camp]].
*[[1951]] &amp;ndash; [[Korean War]]: President [[Harry Truman]] [[Dismissal of General Douglas MacArthur|relieves]] [[General of the Army (United States)|General of the Army]] [[Douglas MacArthur]] of overall command in [[Korea]].
*  1951   &amp;ndash; The [[Stone of Scone]], the stone upon which [[Scotland|Scottish]] monarchs were traditionally crowned, is found on the site of the altar of [[Arbroath Abbey]]. It had been taken by Scottish nationalist students from its place in [[Westminster Abbey]].
*[[1952]] &amp;ndash; The [[Battle of Nanri Island]] takes place.
*[[1955]] &amp;ndash; The [[Air India]] [[Kashmir Princess]] is bombed and crashes in a failed assassination attempt on [[Zhou Enlai]] by the [[Kuomintang]].
*[[1957]] &amp;ndash; United Kingdom agrees to [[Singapore]]an [[Self-governance of Singapore|self-rule]].
*[[1961]] &amp;ndash; The trial of [[Adolf Eichmann]] begins in [[Jerusalem]].
*[[1963]] &amp;ndash; [[Pope John XXIII]] issues ''[[Pacem in terris]]'', the first encyclical addressed to all instead of to Catholics alone.
*[[1965]] &amp;ndash; The [[Palm Sunday tornado outbreak of 1965]]: Fifty-one tornadoes hit in six [[Midwestern]] states, killing 256 people.
*[[1968]] &amp;ndash; President [[Lyndon B. Johnson]] signs the [[Civil Rights Act of 1968]], prohibiting discrimination in the sale, rental, and financing of housing.
*[[1970]] &amp;ndash; ''[[Apollo 13]]'' is launched.
*[[1972]] &amp;ndash; First edition of the [[BBC]] comedy panel game ''[[I'm Sorry I Haven't A Clue]]'' is broadcast, one of the longest running British radio shows in history.
*[[1976]] &amp;ndash; The [[Apple I]] is created.
*[[1977]] &amp;ndash; [[London Transport Executive (GLC)|London Transport]]'s [[Silver Jubilee of Elizabeth II|Silver Jubilee]] [[Routemaster|buses]] are launched.
*[[1979]] &amp;ndash; [[Uganda]]n dictator [[Idi Amin]] is deposed.
*[[1981]] &amp;ndash; A massive [[Brixton riot (1981)|riot]] in [[Brixton]], [[South London]], results in almost 300 police injuries and 65 serious civilian injuries.
*[[1987]] &amp;ndash; The [[Peres-Hussein London Agreement|London Agreement]] is secretly signed between [[Israel]]i Foreign Affairs Minister [[Shimon Peres]] and [[King Hussein]] of [[Jordan]].
*[[1989]] &amp;ndash; [[Ron Hextall]] becomes the first [[goaltender]] in [[National Hockey League|NHL]] history to score a [[goal (ice hockey)|goal]] in the [[Stanley Cup playoffs|playoffs]].
*[[1990]] &amp;ndash; Customs officers in [[Middlesbrough]], England, United Kingdom, say they have seized what they believe to be the barrel of a [[Supergun|massive gun]] on a ship bound for [[Iraq]].
*[[1993]] &amp;ndash; Four hundred fifty [[prison]]ers [[Prison riot|riot]]ed at the [[Southern Ohio Correctional Facility]] in [[Lucasville, Ohio|Lucasville]], [[Ohio]], and continued to do so for ten days, citing grievances related to [[Incarceration in the United States#Conditions|prison conditions]], as well as the forced [[vaccination]] of [[Nation of Islam]] prisoners (for [[tuberculosis]]) against their religious beliefs.
*[[2001]] &amp;ndash; The detained crew of a United States [[EP-3E]] aircraft that [[Hainan Island incident|landed]] in [[Hainan]], [[China]] after a collision with a [[Shenyang J-8|J-8]] fighter, is released.
*[[2002]] &amp;ndash; The [[Ghriba synagogue bombing]] by [[Al Qaeda]] kills 21 in [[Tunisia]].
*  2002   &amp;ndash; [[Venezuelan coup attempt of 2002|Over two hundred thousand people marched]] in Caracas towards the Presidential Palace of Miraflores, to demand the resignation of president Hugo Chávez. 19 of the protesters are killed, and the Minister of Defense Gral. Lucas Rincon announces [[Hugo Chávez]] resignation on national TV.
*[[2006]] &amp;ndash; [[Iranian President]] [[Mahmoud Ahmadinejad]] announces that [[Iran]] has successfully enriched [[uranium]].
*[[2007]] &amp;ndash; [[11 April 2007 Algiers bombings|2007 Algiers bombings]]: Two bombings in the [[Algeria]]n capital of [[Algiers]] kill 33 people and wound a further 222 others.
*[[2011]] &amp;ndash; An [[2011 Minsk Metro bombing|explosion]] in the [[Minsk Metro]], [[Belarus]] kills 15 people and injures 204 others.
*[[2012]] &amp;ndash; An 8.2 magnitude [[April 2012 Aceh earthquake|earthquake]] hits Indonesia, off northern [[Sumatra]] at a depth of 16.4&amp;nbsp;km. A [[tsunami]] hits the island of [[Nias]] at [[Indonesia]].
