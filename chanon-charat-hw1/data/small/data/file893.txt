==Events==
*[[1513]] &amp;ndash; Spanish explorer [[Juan Ponce de León]] first sights land in what is now [[Florida]].
*[[1755]] &amp;ndash; [[Commodore (Royal Navy)|Commodore]] [[William James (naval commander)|William James]] captures the [[Maratha]] fortress of [[Suvarnadurg]] on west coast of India.
*[[1792]] &amp;ndash; The [[Coinage Act of 1792|Coinage Act]] is passed establishing the [[United States Mint]].
*[[1800]] &amp;ndash; [[Ludwig van Beethoven]] leads the premiere of his [[Symphony No. 1 (Beethoven)|First Symphony]] in [[Vienna]].
*[[1801]] &amp;ndash; [[Napoleonic Wars]]: [[Battle of Copenhagen]] &amp;ndash; The [[Kingdom of Great Britain|British]] capture the Danish fleet.
*[[1851]] &amp;ndash; [[Mongkut|Rama IV]] is crowned [[Monarchy of Thailand|King]] of [[Thailand]].
*[[1863]] &amp;ndash; [[Richmond Bread Riot]]: Food shortages incite hundreds of angry women to riot in [[Richmond, Virginia]], and demand that the [[Confederate States of America|Confederate]] government release emergency supplies.
*[[1865]] &amp;ndash; [[American Civil War]]: The [[Siege of Petersburg]] is broken &amp;ndash; [[United States|Union]] troops [[Third Battle of Petersburg|capture the trenches]] around [[Petersburg, Virginia]], forcing Confederate General [[Robert E. Lee]] to retreat.
*  1865   &amp;ndash; American Civil War: [[President of the Confederate States|Confederate President]] [[Jefferson Davis]] and most of his [[Cabinet (government)|Cabinet]] flee the Confederate capital of [[Richmond, Virginia]].
*[[1885]] &amp;ndash; [[Cree]] warriors [[Frog Lake Massacre|attack]] the village of [[Frog Lake, Alberta|Frog Lake, North-West Territories]], Canada, killing nine.
*[[1900]] &amp;ndash; The [[United States Congress]] passes the [[Foraker Act]], giving [[Puerto Rico]] limited [[self-rule]].
*[[1902]] &amp;ndash; [[Dmitry Sipyagin]], [[MVD (Russia)|Minister of Interior]] of the [[Russian Empire]], is assassinated in the [[Marie Palace]], [[St Petersburg]].
*  1902   &amp;ndash; &quot;Electric Theatre&quot;, the first full-time [[movie theater]] in the United States, opens in [[Los Angeles]], [[California]].
*[[1911]] &amp;ndash; The [[Australian Bureau of Statistics]] conducts the country's first [[Census in Australia|national census]].
*[[1912]] &amp;ndash; The ill-fated {{RMS|Titanic}} begins sea trials.
*[[1917]] &amp;ndash; [[World War I]]: United States President [[Woodrow Wilson]] asks the [[Congress of the United States|U.S. Congress]] for a [[declaration of war]] on [[German Empire|Germany]].
*[[1921]] &amp;ndash; The [[Autonomous Government of Khorasan]], a military government encompassing the modern state of [[Iran]], is established.
*[[1930]] &amp;ndash; After the mysterious death of Empress [[Zewditu I|Zewditu]], [[Haile Selassie]] is proclaimed emperor of [[Ethiopia]].
*[[1945]] &amp;ndash; [[Diplomatic relations]] between the [[Soviet Union]] and [[Brazil]] are established.
*[[1956]] &amp;ndash; ''[[As the World Turns]]'' and ''[[The Edge of Night]]'' premiere on [[CBS-TV]]. The two soaps become the first daytime dramas to debut in the 30-minute format.
*[[1962]] &amp;ndash; The first official [[panda crossing]] is opened outside [[London Waterloo station]].
*[[1972]] &amp;ndash; Actor [[Charlie Chaplin]] returns to the United States for the first time since being labeled a [[communism|communist]] during the [[Second Red Scare|Red Scare]] in the early 1950s.
*[[1973]] &amp;ndash; Launch of the [[LexisNexis]] computerized legal research service.
*  1973   &amp;ndash; The [[Liberal Movement (Australia)|Liberal Movement]] breaks away from the [[Liberal and Country League]] in [[South Australia]].
*[[1975]] &amp;ndash; [[Vietnam War]]: Thousands of civilian [[refugee]]s flee from [[Quảng Ngãi Province]] in front of advancing [[North Vietnam]]ese troops.
*  1975   &amp;ndash; Construction of the [[CN Tower]] is completed in Toronto, [[Ontario]], Canada. It reaches {{convert|553.33|m|ft}} in height, becoming the world's tallest free-standing structure.
*[[1980]] &amp;ndash; United States President [[Jimmy Carter]] signs the [[Crude Oil]] [[Windfall Profits Tax]] Act in an effort to help the U.S. economy rebound.
*[[1982]] &amp;ndash; [[Falklands War]]: [[1982 invasion of the Falkland Islands|Argentina invades the Falkland Islands]].
*[[1986]] &amp;ndash; [[Alabama]] governor [[George Wallace]], a former [[segregationist]] most widely known for the &quot;[[Stand in the Schoolhouse Door]]&quot;, announces that he will not seek a fifth four-year term and will retire from public life upon the end of his term in January 1987.
*[[1989]] &amp;ndash; [[Soviet Union|Soviet]] leader [[Mikhail Gorbachev]] arrives in [[Havana]], [[Cuba]] to meet with [[Fidel Castro]] in an attempt to mend strained relations.
*[[1991]] &amp;ndash; [[Rita Johnston]] becomes the first female [[Premier (Canada)|Premier]] of a [[province (Canada)|Canadian province]] when she succeeds [[William Vander Zalm]] (who had resigned) as [[Premier of British Columbia]].
*[[1992]] &amp;ndash; In New York, [[Mafia]] boss [[John Gotti]] is convicted of murder and [[racketeering]] and is later sentenced to life in prison.
*[[1994]] &amp;ndash; The [[National Convention of New Sudan]] of the [[SPLA/M]] opens in [[Chukudum]].
*[[2002]] &amp;ndash; [[Israel]]i forces surround the [[Church of the Nativity]] in [[Bethlehem]] into which armed [[Palestinians]] had retreated; a siege ensues.
*[[2004]] &amp;ndash; [[Islamism|Islamist]] [[terrorism|terrorists]] involved in the [[11 March 2004 Madrid attacks]] attempt to bomb the Spanish high-speed train [[AVE]] near [[Madrid]]; the attack is thwarted.
*[[2006]] &amp;ndash; Over 60 [[tornadoes]] [[April 2, 2006 Central United States tornado outbreak|break out]] in the United States; hardest hit is in [[Tennessee]] with 29 people killed.
*[[2012]] &amp;ndash; A [[Oikos University shooting|mass shooting]] at [[Oikos University]] at [[Oakland, California]] leaves seven people dead and three injured.
*[[2014]] &amp;ndash; A [[2014 Fort Hood shooting|spree shooting]] occurs at the [[Fort Hood]] Army Base near the town of [[Killeen, Texas]], with four people dead, including the gunman, and 16 others sustaining injuries.
