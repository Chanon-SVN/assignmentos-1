==Events==
* [[238]] &amp;ndash; [[Gordian II]] loses the [[Battle of Carthage (238)|Battle of Carthage]] against the [[Numidia]]n forces loyal to [[Maximinus Thrax]] and is killed. [[Gordian I]], his father, commits [[suicide]].
* [[240]] &amp;ndash; [[Shapur I]] is crowned as king of the [[Sasanian Empire]].
* [[467]] &amp;ndash; [[Anthemius]] is elevated to [[Roman emperor|Emperor of the Western Roman Empire]].
* [[627]] &amp;ndash; King [[Edwin of Northumbria]] is converted to [[Christianity]] by [[Paulinus of York|Paulinus]], bishop of [[York]].
*[[1204]] &amp;ndash; The Crusaders of the [[Fourth Crusade]] breach the walls of [[Constantinople]] and [[Siege of Constantinople (1204)|enter the city]], which they completely occupy the following day.
*[[1606]] &amp;ndash; The [[Union Flag]] is adopted as the flag of [[Kingdom of England|English]] and [[Kingdom of Scotland|Scottish]] ships.
*[[1776]] &amp;ndash; [[American Revolution]]: With the [[Halifax Resolves]], the [[North Carolina Provincial Congress]] authorizes its [[Second Continental Congress|Congressional]] delegation to vote for independence from [[Kingdom of Great Britain|Britain]].
*[[1820]] &amp;ndash; [[Alexander Ypsilantis (1792-1828)|Alexander Ypsilantis]] is declared leader of [[Filiki Eteria]], a secret organization to overthrow [[Ottoman Empire|Ottoman]] rule over [[Greece]].
*[[1831]] &amp;ndash; Soldiers marching on the [[Broughton Suspension Bridge]] in [[Manchester]], England cause it to collapse.
*[[1861]] &amp;ndash; [[American Civil War]]: The war begins with [[Confederate States of America|Confederate]] forces firing on [[Fort Sumter]], in the harbor of [[Charleston, South Carolina]].
*[[1862]] &amp;ndash; American Civil War: The Andrews Raid (the [[Great Locomotive Chase]]) occurs, starting from Big Shanty, Georgia (now [[Kennesaw, Georgia|Kennesaw]]).
*[[1864]] &amp;ndash; American Civil War: The [[Fort Pillow massacre]]: Confederate forces kill most of the [[African American]] soldiers that surrendered at [[Fort Pillow]], [[Tennessee]].
*[[1865]] &amp;ndash; American Civil War: [[Mobile, Alabama]], falls to the [[Union (American Civil War)|Union Army]].
*[[1877]] &amp;ndash; The [[United Kingdom of Great Britain and Ireland|United Kingdom]] annexes the [[South African Republic|Transvaal]].
*[[1910]] &amp;ndash; {{SMS|Zrínyi}}, one of the last [[pre-dreadnought battleship]]s built by the [[Austria-Hungary|Austro-Hungarian]] Navy, is launched.
*[[1917]] &amp;ndash; [[World War I]]: [[Canada|Canadian]] forces successfully complete the taking of [[Battle of Vimy Ridge|Vimy Ridge]] from the [[Germans]].
*[[1927]] &amp;ndash; [[April 12 Incident]]: [[Chiang Kai-shek]] orders the [[Communist Party of China]] members executed in [[Shanghai]], ending the [[First United Front (China)|First United Front]].
*[[1928]] &amp;ndash; The [[Bremen (aircraft)|Bremen]], a German [[Junkers W33]] type aircraft, takes off for the first successful transatlantic aeroplane flight from east to west.
*[[1934]] &amp;ndash; The strongest surface wind gust in the world at 231&amp;nbsp;mph, is measured on the summit of [[Mount Washington (New Hampshire)|Mount Washington]], [[New Hampshire]].
*  1934   &amp;ndash; The U.S. [[Auto-Lite Strike]] begins, culminating in a five-day melee between [[Ohio National Guard]] troops and 6,000 [[strike action|strikers]] and picketers.
*[[1935]] &amp;ndash; First flight of the [[Bristol Blenheim]].
*[[1937]] &amp;ndash; Sir [[Frank Whittle]] ground-tests the first [[jet engine]] designed to power an aircraft, at [[Rugby, Warwickshire|Rugby, England]].
*[[1945]] &amp;ndash; U.S. [[President of the United States|President]] [[Franklin D. Roosevelt]] dies while in office; vice-president [[Harry Truman]] is sworn in as the 33rd President.
*[[1955]] &amp;ndash; The [[polio vaccine]], developed by Dr. [[Jonas Salk]], is declared [[Jonas Salk#Test results announced|safe and effective]].
*[[1961]] &amp;ndash; The [[Soviet Union|Soviet]] [[cosmonaut]] [[Yuri Gagarin]] becomes the first human to travel into [[outer space]] and perform the first manned [[orbit]]al flight, in [[Vostok spacecraft|Vostok]] 3KA-2 ([[Vostok 1]]).
*[[1963]] &amp;ndash; The [[Soviet Union|Soviet]] nuclear-powered submarine [[Soviet submarine K-33|K-33]] collides with the [[Finland|Finnish]] merchant vessel M/S ''Finnclipper'' in the [[Denmark|Danish]] straits.
*[[1970]] &amp;ndash; [[Soviet submarine K-8]], carrying four nuclear torpedoes, sinks in the [[Bay of Biscay]] four days after a fire on board.
*[[1980]] &amp;ndash; [[Samuel Doe]] takes control of [[Liberia]] in a ''[[coup d'état]]'', ending over 130 years of minority [[Americo-Liberian]] rule over the country.
*  1980   &amp;ndash; [[Terry Fox]] begins his &quot;[[Marathon of Hope]]&quot; at [[St. John's, Newfoundland and Labrador|St. John's, Newfoundland]].
*[[1981]] &amp;ndash; The first launch of a [[Space Shuttle]] (''[[Space Shuttle Columbia|Columbia]]'') takes place - the [[STS-1]] mission.
*[[1990]] &amp;ndash; [[Jim Gary]]'s &quot;Twentieth Century Dinosaurs&quot; exhibition opens at the [[Smithsonian Institution]] [[National Museum of Natural History]] in [[Washington, D.C.]]. He is the only sculptor ever invited to present a [[solo show|solo exhibition]] there. 
*[[1992]] &amp;ndash; The ''Euro Disney Resort'' officially opens with its theme park ''[[Disneyland Park (Paris)|Euro Disneyland]]''. The resort and its park's name are subsequently changed to [[Disneyland Paris]].
*[[1994]] &amp;ndash; [[Canter &amp; Siegel]] post the first commercial mass [[Usenet]] [[Newsgroup spam|spam]].
*[[1998]] &amp;ndash; An [[earthquake]] in [[Slovenia]], measuring 5.6 on the [[Richter magnitude scale|Richter scale]] occurs near the town of [[Bovec]].
*[[1999]] &amp;ndash; US President [[Bill Clinton]] is cited for [[contempt of court]] for giving &quot;intentionally false statements&quot; in a [[sexual harassment]] [[civil lawsuit]].
*[[2002]] &amp;ndash; A female [[suicide bomber]] blows herself up at the entrance to Jerusalem's [[Mahane Yehuda]] open-air market, killing 7 and wounding 104.
*[[2007]] &amp;ndash; A suicide bomber [[2007 Iraqi Parliament bombing|penetrates the Green Zone and detonates]] in a cafeteria within a parliament building, killing Iraqi MP [[Mohammed Awad]] and wounding more than twenty other people.
*[[2009]] &amp;ndash; [[Zimbabwe]] officially abandons the [[Zimbabwe Dollar]] as its official currency.
*[[2010]] &amp;ndash; A [[2010 Merano train derailment|train derails]] near [[Merano]], [[Italy]], after running into a [[landslide]], causing nine deaths and injuring 28 people.
*[[2014]] &amp;ndash; A [[2014 Valparaíso wildfire|wildfire]] ravages the Chilean city of [[Valparaíso]], killing 16, displacing nearly 10,000, and destroying over 2,000 homes.
