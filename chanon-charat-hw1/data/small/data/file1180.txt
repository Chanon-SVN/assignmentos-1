==Events==
*[[823]] &amp;ndash; [[Lothair I]] is crowned [[King of Italy]] by [[Pope Paschal I]].
*[[1081]] &amp;ndash; [[Alexios I Komnenos]] is crowned [[Byzantine emperor]] at [[Constantinople]], bringing the [[Komnenian dynasty]] to full power.
*[[1242]] &amp;ndash; During a [[Battle of the Ice|battle on the ice]] of [[Lake Peipus]], [[Republic of Novgorod|Russian]] forces, led by [[Alexander Nevsky]], rebuff an invasion attempt by the [[Teutonic Knights]].
*[[1536]] &amp;ndash; [[Royal Entry]] of [[Charles V, Holy Roman Emperor|Charles V]] into [[Rome]]: the last [[Roman triumph]].
*[[1566]] &amp;ndash; Two-hundred [[Habsburg Netherlands|Dutch]] noblemen, led by [[Hendrik van Brederode]], force themselves into the presence of [[Margaret of Parma]] and present the [[Compromise of Nobles|Petition of Compromise]], denouncing the [[Spanish Inquisition]] in the Netherlands. The Inquisition is suspended and a delegation is sent to [[Habsburg Spain|Spain]] to petition [[Philip II of Spain|Philip II]].
*[[1609]] &amp;ndash; ''Daimyo'' (Lord) of the [[Satsuma Domain]] in southern [[Kyūshū]], [[Tokugawa Shogunate|Japan]], completes [[Invasion of Ryukyu|his successful invasion]] of the [[Ryūkyū Kingdom]] in [[Okinawa]].
*[[1614]] &amp;ndash; In [[Virginia]], [[Native Americans in the United States|Native American]] [[Pocahontas]] marries [[English People|English]] colonist [[John Rolfe]].
*[[1621]] &amp;ndash; The ''[[Mayflower]]'' sets sail from [[Plymouth, Massachusetts]] on a return trip to [[England]].
*[[1710]] &amp;ndash; The [[Statute of Anne]] receives the [[Royal Assent]] establishing the [[Copyright law of the United Kingdom]].
*[[1722]] &amp;ndash; The Dutch explorer [[Jacob Roggeveen]] discovers [[Easter Island]].
*[[1792]] &amp;ndash; [[President of the United States|U.S. President]] [[George Washington]] exercises his authority to [[veto]] a bill, the first time this power is used in the [[United States]].
*[[1804]] &amp;ndash; [[High Possil Meteorite]]: The first recorded [[meteorite]] in [[Scotland]] falls in [[Possil]].
*[[1818]] &amp;ndash; In the [[Battle of Maipú]], [[Chile]]'s independence movement &amp;ndash; led by [[Bernardo O'Higgins]] and [[José de San Martín]] &amp;ndash; win a decisive victory over Spain, leaving 2,000 Spaniards and 1,000 Chilean patriots dead.
*[[1847]] &amp;ndash; [[Birkenhead Park]], the first civic public park in Britain, is opened in [[Birkenhead]].
*[[1862]] &amp;ndash; [[American Civil War]]: The [[Battle of Yorktown (1862)|Battle of Yorktown]] begins.
*[[1879]] &amp;ndash; [[Chile]] declares war on [[Bolivia]] and [[Peru]], starting the [[War of the Pacific]].
*[[1900]] &amp;ndash; Archaeologists in [[Knossos]], [[Crete]], discover a large cache of [[clay tablet]]s with [[hieroglyph]]ic writing in a script they call [[Linear B]].
*[[1904]] &amp;ndash; The first international [[rugby league]] match is played between [[England national rugby league team|England]] and an Other Nationalities team (Welsh &amp; Scottish players) in [[Central Park (Wigan)|Central Park]], [[Wigan]], England.
*[[1922]] &amp;ndash; The [[American Birth Control League]], forerunner of [[Planned Parenthood]], is incorporated.
*[[1923]] &amp;ndash; [[Firestone Tire and Rubber Company]] begins production of balloon-[[tire]]s.
*[[1932]] &amp;ndash; Alcohol [[prohibition]] in [[Finland]] ends. Alcohol sales begin in [[Alko]] liquor stores.
*  1932   &amp;ndash; [[Dominion of Newfoundland]]: 10,000 rioters seize the [[Colonial Building]] leading to the end of self-government.
*[[1933]] &amp;ndash; U.S. President [[Franklin D. Roosevelt]] signs two [[executive order (United States)|executive order]]s: 6101 to establish the [[Civilian Conservation Corps]], and [[Executive Order 6102|6102]] &quot;forbidding the Hoarding of Gold Coin, Gold Bullion, and Gold Certificates&quot; by U.S. citizens.
*[[1936]] &amp;ndash; [[Tupelo-Gainesville tornado outbreak]]: An [[Fujita scale|F5]] tornado kills 233 in [[Tupelo, Mississippi]].
*[[1942]] &amp;ndash; [[World War II]]: The [[Imperial Japanese Navy]] launches [[Easter Sunday Raid|a carrier-based air attack on Colombo]], [[Ceylon]] during the [[Indian Ocean Raid]]. Port and civilian facilities are damaged and the [[Royal Navy]] [[cruisers]] {{HMS|Cornwall|56|6}} and {{HMS|Dorsetshire|40|6}} are sunk southwest of the island.
*[[1943]] &amp;ndash; World War II: American bomber aircraft accidentally cause more than 900 civilian deaths, including 209 children, and 1,300 wounded among the civilian population of the [[Belgian]] town of [[Mortsel]]. Their target was the Erla factory one kilometer from the residential area hit.
*[[1944]] &amp;ndash; World War II: 270 inhabitants of the Greek town of [[Kleisoura, Kastoria|Kleisoura]] are executed by the [[Nazi Germany|Germans]].
*[[1945]] &amp;ndash; [[Cold War]]: [[Socialist Federal Republic of Yugoslavia|Yugoslav]] leader [[Josip Broz Tito]] signs an agreement with the [[Soviet Union]] to allow &quot;temporary entry of Soviet troops into Yugoslav territory&quot;.
*[[1946]] &amp;ndash; Soviet troops leave the island of [[Bornholm]], [[Denmark]] after an 11-month occupation.
*[[1949]] &amp;ndash; [[Fireside Theater]] debuts on [[television]].
*  1949   &amp;ndash; A fire in a hospital in [[Effingham, Illinois]], kills 77 people and leads to nationwide fire code improvements in the [[United States]].
*[[1951]] &amp;ndash; [[Ethel and Julius Rosenberg]] are sentenced to death for [[espionage|spying]] for the Soviet Union.
*[[1956]] &amp;ndash; [[Fidel Castro]] declares himself at war with [[President of Cuba|Cuban President]] [[Fulgencio Batista]].
*  1956   &amp;ndash; In [[Sri Lanka]], the [[Mahajana Eksath Peramuna]] win the [[general elections]] in a landslide and [[Solomon Bandaranaike|S.W.R.D. Bandaranaike]] is sworn in as the [[Prime Minister]].
*[[1957]] &amp;ndash; In [[India]], [[Communist Party of India|Communists]] win the first elections in united [[Kerala]] and [[E.M.S. Namboodiripad]] is sworn in as the first [[chief minister]].
*[[1958]] &amp;ndash; [[Ripple Rock]], an underwater threat to navigation in the [[Seymour Narrows]] in [[Canada]] is destroyed in one of the largest non-nuclear controlled explosions of the time.
*[[1969]] &amp;ndash; [[Vietnam War]]: Massive [[Opposition to the Vietnam War|antiwar demonstration]]s occur in many U.S. cities.
*[[1971]] &amp;ndash; In Sri Lanka, [[Janatha Vimukthi Peramuna]] launches a revolt against the [[United Front]] government of [[Sirimavo Bandaranaike]].
*[[1976]] &amp;ndash; In the [[China|People's Republic of China]], the [[April Fifth Movement]] leads to the [[Tiananmen incident]].
*[[1986]] &amp;ndash; Three people are killed in the [[1986 Berlin discotheque bombing|bombing]] of the [[La Belle (discotheque)|La Belle Discothèque]] in [[West Berlin]], [[Germany]].
*[[1991]] &amp;ndash; An [[Atlantic Southeast Airlines|ASA]] [[Embraer EMB 120|EMB 120]] crashes in [[Brunswick, Georgia]], killing all 23 aboard including [[John Tower|Sen. John Tower]] and [[Sonny Carter|Astronaut Sonny Carter]].
*[[1992]] &amp;ndash; [[Alberto Fujimori]], president of Peru, [[1992 Peruvian constitutional crisis|dissolves the Peruvian congress]] by military force.
*  1992   &amp;ndash; The [[Siege of Sarajevo]] begins when [[Serbs|Serb]] [[Paramilitary|paramilitaries]] murder peace protesters [[Suada Dilberovic]] and [[Olga Sučić]] on the Vrbanja Bridge.
*[[1994]] &amp;ndash; American musician [[Death_of_Kurt_Cobain|Kurt Cobain commits suicide]]. 
*[[1998]] &amp;ndash; In [[Japan]], the [[Akashi Kaikyō Bridge]] linking [[Awaji Island]] with [[Honshū]] and costing about $3.8 billion [[United States dollar|USD]], opens to traffic, becoming the largest [[suspension bridge]] in the world.
*[[1999]] &amp;ndash; Two [[History of Libya under Muammar Gaddafi|Libyans]] suspected of bringing down [[Pan Am flight 103]] in [[1988]] are handed over for eventual trial in the Netherlands.
*[[2009]] &amp;ndash; [[North Korea]] launches its controversial [[Kwangmyŏngsŏng-2]] rocket. The satellite passed over mainland [[Japan]], which prompted an immediate reaction from the [[United Nations Security Council]], as well as participating states of [[Six-party talks]].
*[[2010]] &amp;ndash; Twenty-nine coal miners are killed in an explosion at the [[Upper Big Branch Mine disaster|Upper Big Branch Mine]] in [[West Virginia]].
