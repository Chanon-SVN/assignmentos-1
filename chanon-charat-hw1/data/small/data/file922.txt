==Events==
*[[1025]] &amp;ndash; [[Bolesław I Chrobry|Bolesław Chrobry]] is crowned in [[Gniezno]], becoming the first [[List of Polish monarchs|King]] of [[Poland]].
*[[1506]] &amp;ndash; The [[cornerstone]] of the current [[St. Peter's Basilica]] is laid.
*[[1518]] &amp;ndash; [[Bona Sforza]] is crowned as [[queen consort]] of Poland.
*[[1521]] &amp;ndash; Trial of [[Martin Luther]] begins its second day during the assembly of the [[Diet of Worms]]. He refuses to recant his [[Lutheranism|teachings]] despite the risk of excommunication.
*[[1689]] &amp;ndash; [[Boston]]ians rise up [[1689 Boston revolt|in rebellion]] against Sir [[Edmund Andros]].
*[[1738]] &amp;ndash; ''[[Real Academia de la Historia]]'' (&quot;Royal Academy of History&quot;) is founded in [[Madrid]].
*[[1775]] &amp;ndash; [[American Revolution]]: The [[Battles of Lexington and Concord|British advancement by sea]] begins; [[Paul Revere]] and other riders warn the countryside of the troop movements.
*[[1797]] &amp;ndash; The [[Battle of Neuwied (1797)|Battle of Neuwied]] &amp;ndash; [[France|French]] victory against the [[Austria]]ns.
*[[1807]] &amp;ndash; The [[Harwich ferry disaster]] occurred near the North Sea port of [[Harwich]] on the [[Essex]] coast ([[England]]) in which 60-90 people drowned during the capsizing of a small ferry boat.
*[[1831]] &amp;ndash; The [[University of Alabama]] is founded.
*[[1848]] &amp;ndash; American victory at the [[battle of Cerro Gordo]] opens the way for invasion of [[Mexico]].
*[[1857]] &amp;ndash; &quot;[[The Spirits Book]]&quot; by [[Allan Kardec]] is published, marking the birth of [[Spiritualism]] in [[France]].
*[[1864]] &amp;ndash; [[Battle of Dybbøl]]: A [[Kingdom of Prussia|Prussian]]-[[Austrian Empire|Austrian]] army defeats [[Denmark]] and gains control of [[Duchy of Schleswig|Schleswig]]. Denmark surrenders the province in the following peace settlement.
*[[1880]] &amp;ndash; An [[Fujita scale|F4]] [[tornado]] strikes [[Marshfield, Missouri]], killing 99 people and injuring 100.
*[[1881]] &amp;ndash; [[Billy the Kid]] escapes from the [[Lincoln County, New Mexico|Lincoln County]] jail in [[Mesilla]], [[New Mexico]].
*[[1897]] &amp;ndash; The [[Greco-Turkish War (1897)|Greco-Turkish War]] is declared between [[Greece]] and the [[Ottoman Empire]].
*[[1899]] &amp;ndash; The [[St. Andrew's Ambulance Association]] is granted a [[Royal Charter]] by [[Queen Victoria]].
*[[1902]] &amp;ndash; [[Quetzaltenango]], the second largest city of [[Guatemala]], is [[1902 Guatemala earthquake|destroyed by an earthquake]].
*[[1906]] &amp;ndash; [[1906 San Francisco earthquake|An earthquake]] and fire destroy much of [[San Francisco]], [[California]].
*[[1909]] &amp;ndash; [[Joan of Arc]] is beatified in [[Rome]].
*[[1912]] &amp;ndash; The [[Cunard Line|Cunard]] liner {{RMS|Carpathia|}} brings 705 survivors from the {{RMS|Titanic|}} to [[New York City]].
*[[1915]] &amp;ndash; [[France|French]] pilot [[Roland Garros (aviator)|Roland Garros]] is shot down and glides to a landing on the [[Germany|German]] side of the lines during [[World War I]].
*[[1923]] &amp;ndash; [[Yankee Stadium (1923)|Yankee Stadium]], &quot;The House that [[Babe Ruth|Ruth]] Built&quot;, opens.
*[[1924]] &amp;ndash; [[Simon &amp; Schuster]] publishes the first [[crossword]] puzzle book.
*[[1930]] &amp;ndash; [[BBC]] reported there was no news, then played out with piano music.
*[[1936]] &amp;ndash; The first [[Champions Day]] is celebrated in [[Detroit]], [[Michigan]].
*[[1942]] &amp;ndash; [[World War II]]: The [[Doolittle Raid]] on [[Japan]]. [[Tokyo]], [[Yokohama]], [[Kobe]] and [[Nagoya]] are bombed.
*[[1942]] &amp;ndash; [[Pierre Laval]] becomes [[Prime Minister]] of [[Vichy France]].
*[[1943]] &amp;ndash; World War II: [[Operation Vengeance]], Admiral [[Isoroku Yamamoto]] is killed when his aircraft is shot down by U.S. fighters over [[Bougainville Island]].
*[[1945]] &amp;ndash; Over 1,000 [[bomber]]s attack the small island of [[Heligoland]], [[Germany]].
*[[1946]] &amp;ndash; The [[International Court of Justice]] holds its inaugural meeting in [[The Hague]], [[Netherlands]].
*[[1949]] &amp;ndash; The keel for the aircraft carrier {{USS|United States|CVA-58|6}} is laid down at [[Northrop Grumman Newport News|Newport News Drydock and Shipbuilding]]. However, construction is canceled five days later, resulting in the [[Revolt of the Admirals]].
*[[1954]] &amp;ndash; [[Gamal Abdal Nasser]] seizes power in [[Egypt]].
*[[1955]] &amp;ndash; Twenty-nine nations meet at [[Bandung]], [[Indonesia]], for the first [[Asian-African Conference]].
*[[1958]] &amp;ndash; A [[United States]] federal court rules that [[poet]] [[Ezra Pound]] be released from an [[insane asylum]].
*[[1961]] &amp;ndash; The [[Vienna Convention on Diplomatic Relations]], a cornerstone of modern international relations, is adopted.
*  1961   &amp;ndash; [[Conferência das Organizações Nacionalistas das Colónias Portuguesas|CONCP]] is founded in [[Casablanca]] as a united front of [[African]] movements opposing [[Portugal|Portuguese]] colonial rule.
*[[1974]] &amp;ndash; The [[Prime Minister]] of [[Pakistan]] [[Zulfikar Ali Bhutto]] inaugurates [[Lahore]]'s [[dry port]].
*[[1980]] &amp;ndash; The [[Zimbabwe|Republic of Zimbabwe]] (formerly [[Rhodesia]]) comes into being, with [[Canaan Banana]] as the country's first [[List of Presidents of Zimbabwe|President]]. The [[Zimbabwe Dollar]] replaces the [[Rhodesian Dollar]] as the official currency.
*[[1981]] &amp;ndash; The [[longest professional baseball game]] is begun in [[Pawtucket, Rhode Island|Pawtucket]], [[Rhode Island]]. The game is suspended at 4:00 the next morning and finally completed on [[June 23]].
*[[1983]] &amp;ndash; A [[suicide bomber]] [[April 1983 U.S. Embassy bombing|destroys the United States embassy]] in [[Beirut]], [[Lebanon]], killing 63 people.
*[[1988]] &amp;ndash; The United States launches [[Operation Praying Mantis]] against [[Iran]]ian naval forces in the largest naval battle since World War II.
*[[1992]] &amp;ndash; General [[Abdul Rashid Dostum]] revolts against [[President of Afghanistan|President]] [[Mohammad Najibullah]] of the [[Democratic Republic of Afghanistan]] and allies with [[Ahmed Shah Massoud]] to capture [[Kabul]].
*[[1996]] &amp;ndash; In Lebanon, at least 106 civilians are [[1996 shelling of Qana|killed]] when the [[Israel Defense Forces]] shell the [[United Nations]] compound at Quana where more than 800 civilians had taken refuge.
*[[2007]] &amp;ndash; The [[Supreme Court of the United States]] upholds the [[Partial-Birth Abortion Ban Act]] in a 5–4 decision.
*  2007   &amp;ndash; A [[18 April 2007 Baghdad bombings|series of bombings]], two of them being [[suicide bomber|suicides]], occur in [[Baghdad]], killing 198 and injuring 251.
*[[2013]] &amp;ndash; A [[18 April 2013 Baghdad bombing|suicide bombing]] in a [[Baghdad]] cafe kills 27 people and injures another 65.
*[[2014]] &amp;ndash; 16 people are killed in an [[2014 Mount Everest avalanche|avalanche]] on [[Mount Everest]].
