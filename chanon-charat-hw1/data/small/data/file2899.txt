'''Bruin''' is folk term used for brown bears (from the Dutch ''bruin'' meaning brown). '''Bruin''' or '''Bruins''' or '''BRUIN''' may refer to the following:

*Bruin, the [[brown bear]] in the [[Reynard cycle]] fables, whence the word was introduced into English.
