==Events==
*[[33 BC]] &amp;ndash; [[Lucius Marcius Philippus (consul 38 BC)|Lucius Marcius Philippus]], step-brother to the future emperor [[Augustus]], celebrates a [[Roman triumph|triumph]] for his victories while serving as governor in one of the provinces of [[Hispania]].
* [[395]] &amp;ndash; Emperor [[Arcadius]] marries [[Aelia Eudoxia]], daughter of the [[Franks|Frankish]] general [[Flavius Bauto]]. She becomes one of the more powerful Roman empresses of [[Late Antiquity]].
* [[629]] &amp;ndash; [[Shahrbaraz]] is crowned as king of the [[Sasanian Empire]].
*[[1296]] &amp;ndash; [[First War of Scottish Independence]]: [[John Balliol]]'s [[Scotland|Scottish]] army is defeated by an [[Kingdom of England|English]] army commanded by [[John de Warenne, 6th Earl of Surrey]] at the [[Battle of Dunbar (1296)|Battle of Dunbar]].
*[[1509]] &amp;ndash; [[Pope Julius II]] places the [[Italy|Italian]] state of [[Republic of Venice|Venice]] under [[interdict]].
*[[1521]] &amp;ndash; [[Battle of Mactan]]: Explorer [[Ferdinand Magellan]] is killed by natives in the [[Philippines]] led by chief [[Lapu-Lapu]].
*[[1522]] &amp;ndash; Combined forces of [[Habsburg Spain|Spain]] and the [[Papal States]] defeat a [[Kingdom of France|French]] and [[Republic of Venice|Venetian]] army at the [[Battle of Bicocca]].
*[[1539]] &amp;ndash; Re-founding of the city of [[Bogotá]], [[New Kingdom of Granada|New Granada]] (now [[Colombia]]), by [[Nikolaus Federmann]] and [[Sebastián de Belalcázar]].
*[[1565]] &amp;ndash; [[Cebu City|Cebu]] is established becoming the first [[Spain|Spanish]] settlement in the [[Philippines]].
*[[1578]] &amp;ndash; [[Duel of the Mignons]] claims the lives of two [[favourite]]s of [[Henry III of France]] and two favorites of [[Henry I, Duke of Guise]].
*[[1595]] &amp;ndash; The relics of [[Saint Sava]] are incinerated in [[Belgrade]] by the Ottomans, where today [[Church of Saint Sava|the largest Orthodox church building in the world]] stands
*[[1650]] &amp;ndash; The [[Battle of Carbisdale]]: A [[Cavalier|Royalist]] army from [[Orkney]] invades mainland [[Scotland]] but is defeated by a [[Covenanter]] army.
*[[1667]] &amp;ndash; The blind and impoverished [[John Milton]] sells the copyright of ''[[Paradise Lost]]'' for [[Pound sterling|£]]10.
*[[1749]] &amp;ndash; First performance of [[George Frideric Handel]]'s ''[[Music for the Royal Fireworks]]'' in [[Green Park|Green Park, London]].
*[[1777]] &amp;ndash; [[American Revolutionary War]]: The [[Battle of Ridgefield]]: A [[Great Britain|British]] invasion force engages and defeats [[Continental Army]] regulars and [[militia]] irregulars at [[Ridgefield, Connecticut]].
*[[1805]] &amp;ndash; [[First Barbary War]]: [[United States Marines]] and [[Berber people|Berbers]] attack the [[Tripoli]]tan city of [[Derna, Libya|Derna]] (The &quot;shores of Tripoli&quot; part of the [[Marines' hymn]]).
*[[1810]] &amp;ndash; [[Ludwig van Beethoven|Beethoven]] composes ''[[Für Elise]]''.
*[[1813]] &amp;ndash; [[War of 1812]]: [[United States|American]] troops capture the [[Capital (political)|capital]] of [[Upper Canada]] in the [[Battle of York]] (present day [[Toronto]], [[Canada]]).
*[[1840]] &amp;ndash; Foundation stone for new [[Palace of Westminster]], [[London]], is laid by wife of Sir [[Charles Barry]].
*[[1861]] &amp;ndash; [[President of the United States|American President]] [[Abraham Lincoln]] suspends the writ of ''[[habeas corpus]]''.
*[[1865]] &amp;ndash; The [[New York State Senate]] creates [[Cornell University]] as the [[New York|state]]'s [[Morrill Land-Grant Colleges Act|land grant]] institution.
*  1865   &amp;ndash; The [[steamboat]] {{ss|Sultana||}}, carrying 2,400 passengers, explodes and sinks in the [[Mississippi River]], killing 1,700, most of whom are [[Union (American Civil War)|Union]] survivors of the [[Andersonville Prison|Andersonville]] and [[Cahaba Prison]]s.
*[[1904]] &amp;ndash; The [[Australian Labor Party]] becomes the first such party to gain [[Central government|national government]], under [[Chris Watson]].
*[[1909]] &amp;ndash; [[Sultan]] of [[Ottoman Empire]] [[Abdul Hamid II]] is overthrown, and is succeeded by his brother, [[Mehmed V]].
*[[1911]] &amp;ndash; Following the resignation and death of [[William P. Frye]], a [[Presidents pro tempore of the United States Senate, 1911-1913|compromise is reached to rotate]] the office of [[President pro tempore of the United States Senate]].
*[[1914]] &amp;ndash; [[Honduras]] becomes a signatory to the [[Buenos Aires Convention|Buenos Aires copyright treaty]].
*[[1927]] &amp;ndash; [[Carabineros de Chile]] ([[Chile]]an national [[police]] force and [[gendarmery]]) are created.
*[[1936]] &amp;ndash; The [[United Auto Workers]] (UAW) gains autonomy from the [[American Federation of Labor]].
*[[1941]] &amp;ndash; [[World War II]]: [[Germany|German]] troops [[Axis occupation of Greece|enter Athens]].
*  1941   &amp;ndash; World War II: The Communist Party of Slovenia, the Slovene Christian Socialists, the left-wing Slovene Sokols (also known as &quot;National Democrats&quot;) and a group of progressive intellectuals establish the [[Liberation Front of the Slovenian People]].
*[[1945]] &amp;ndash; World War II: German troops are finally expelled from [[Lapland War|Finnish Lapland]].
*  1945   &amp;ndash; World War II: [[Benito Mussolini]] is arrested by [[Italian resistance movement|Italian partisans]] in [[Dongo (CO)|Dongo]], while attempting escape disguised as a German soldier.
*[[1950]] &amp;ndash; [[Apartheid]]: In [[South Africa]], the [[Group Areas Act]] is passed formally segregating races.
*[[1953]] &amp;ndash; [[Operation Moolah]] is initiated by U.S. [[General]] [[Mark W. Clark]] against Communist pilots in the [[Korean War]].
*[[1960]] &amp;ndash; [[Togo]] gains independence from [[France|French]]-administered [[United Nations Trust Territories|UN trusteeship]].
*[[1961]] &amp;ndash; [[Sierra Leone]] is granted its independence from the [[United Kingdom]], with [[Milton Margai]] as the first [[Heads of Government of Sierra Leone|Prime Minister]].
*[[1967]] &amp;ndash; [[Expo 67]] officially opens in [[Montreal]], [[Canada]] with a large opening ceremony broadcast around the world.  It opens to the public the next day.
*[[1974]] &amp;ndash; Ten thousand march in [[Washington, D.C.]], calling for the impeachment of U.S. President [[Richard Nixon]]
*[[1977]] &amp;ndash; Twenty-eight people are killed in the [[Guatemala City air disaster]].
*[[1978]] &amp;ndash; Former United States President Nixon aide [[John D. Ehrlichman]] is released from an Arizona prison after serving 18 months for [[Watergate scandal|Watergate]]-related crimes.
*[[1981]] &amp;ndash; [[Xerox PARC]] introduces the [[computer mouse]].
* [[1986]] &amp;ndash; The City of [[Prypiat]] as well as the surrounding areas are evacuated due to [[Chernobyl Disaster]]
*[[1987]] &amp;ndash; The [[United States Department of Justice|U.S. Department of Justice]] bars [[Austria]]n President [[Kurt Waldheim]] from entering the [[United States]], saying he had aided in the deportation and execution of thousands of [[Jews]] and others as a German Army officer during [[World War II]].
*[[1989]] &amp;ndash; The [[April 27 Demonstration]],a student-led protest responding to the [[April 26 Editorial]], during the [[Tiananmen Square protests of 1989]].
*[[1992]] &amp;ndash; The [[Federal Republic of Yugoslavia]], comprising [[Serbia]] and [[Montenegro]], is proclaimed.
*  1992   &amp;ndash; [[Betty Boothroyd]] becomes the first woman to be elected [[Speaker of the House of Commons (United Kingdom)|Speaker]] of the [[British House of Commons]] in its 700-year history.
*  1992   &amp;ndash; The [[Russia|Russian Federation]] and 12 other former [[Soviet Union|Soviet]] [[Republics of the Soviet Union|republics]] become members of the [[International Monetary Fund]] and the [[World Bank]].
*[[1993]] &amp;ndash; All members of the [[Zambia national football team]] lose their lives in a plane crash off [[Libreville]], [[Gabon]] en route to [[Dakar]], [[Senegal]] to play a [[1994 FIFA World Cup]] [[1994 FIFA World Cup qualification (CAF)|qualifying match]] against [[Senegal national football team|Senegal]].
*[[1994]] &amp;ndash; [[South African general election, 1994]]: The first democratic [[general election]] in [[South Africa]], in which black citizens could vote. The [[South African Constitution of 1993|Interim Constitution]] comes into force.
*[[1996]] &amp;ndash; The [[1996 Lebanon war]] ends.
*[[2002]] &amp;ndash; The last successful telemetry from the [[NASA]] space probe [[Pioneer 10]].
*[[2005]] &amp;ndash; The superjumbo jet aircraft [[Airbus A380]] makes its first flight from [[Toulouse]], [[France]].
*[[2006]] &amp;ndash; Construction begins on the [[One World Trade Center|Freedom Tower]] for the new [[World Trade Center]] in [[New York City]].
*[[2007]] &amp;ndash; [[Estonia]]n authorities remove the [[Bronze Soldier of Tallinn|Bronze Soldier]], a [[Soviet Union|Soviet]] [[Red Army]] [[war memorial]] in [[Tallinn]], amid political controversy with [[Russia]].
*[[2011]] &amp;ndash; The [[April 25–28, 2011 tornado outbreak]] devastates parts of the [[Southeastern United States]], especially the states of [[Alabama]], [[Mississippi]], [[Georgia (U.S. state)|Georgia]], and [[Tennessee]]. 205 tornadoes touched down on April 27 alone, killing more than 300 and injuring hundreds more.
*[[2012]] &amp;ndash; At least [[2012 Dnipropetrovsk explosions|four explosions]] hit the [[Ukraine|Ukrainian]] city of [[Dnipropetrovsk]] with at least 27 people injured.
*[[2014]] &amp;ndash; Popes [[Pope John XXIII|John XXIII]] and [[Pope John Paul II|John Paul II]] are declared [[saint]]s in the first papal [[Canonization of Pope John XXIII and Pope John Paul II|canonization]] since 1954.
*  2014   &amp;ndash; A [[April 27–30, 2014 tornado outbreak|tornado outbreak]] over much of the eastern [[United States]] kills more than 45 people.
