==Events==
*[[1140]] &amp;ndash; [[Song Dynasty]] general [[Yue Fei]] defeats an army led by [[Jin Dynasty (1115–1234)|Jin Dynasty]] general [[Wanyan Wuzhu]] at the [[Battle of Yancheng]] during the [[Jurchen campaigns against the Song Dynasty|Jin–Song wars]].
*[[1192]] &amp;ndash; [[Minamoto no Yoritomo]] becomes [[Shogun|Seii Tai Shōgun]] and the ''[[de facto]]'' ruler of [[Japan]]. (Traditional [[Japanese calendar|Japanese date]]: July 12, 1192)
*[[1331]] &amp;ndash; King [[Stephen Uroš III Dečanski of Serbia|Stephen Uroš III]], after months of anarchy, surrenders to his son and rival [[Stephen Uroš IV Dušan of Serbia|Stephen Dušan]], who succeeds as [[Kingdom of Serbia (medieval)|King of Serbia]].
*[[1680]] &amp;ndash; [[Puebloan peoples|Pueblo Indians]] capture [[Santa Fe, New Mexico|Santa Fe]] from the [[Spain|Spanish]] during the [[Pueblo Revolt]].
*[[1689]] &amp;ndash; The [[Battle of Dunkeld]] in [[Scotland]].
*[[1770]] &amp;ndash; [[James Cook]] formally claims eastern [[Australia]] for [[Kingdom of Great Britain|Great Britain]], naming it [[New South Wales]].
*[[1772]] &amp;ndash; King [[Gustav III of Sweden|Gustav III]] completes his [[coup d'état]] by adopting a new [[Swedish Constitution of 1772|Constitution]], ending [[Age of Liberty|half a century of parliamentary rule]] in [[Sweden]] and installing himself as an [[Enlightened absolutism|enlightened despot]].
*[[1778]] &amp;ndash; [[American Revolutionary War]]: British forces [[Siege of Pondicherry (1778)|begin besieging]] the French outpost at [[Puducherry|Pondichéry]].
*[[1808]] &amp;ndash; [[Battle of Vimeiro]]: [[United Kingdom of Great Britain and Ireland|British]] and [[Portuguese people|Portuguese]] forces led by General [[Arthur Wellesley, 1st Duke of Wellington|Arthur Wellesley]] defeat [[French Armed Forces|French]] force under Major-General [[Jean-Andoche Junot]] near the village of [[Vimeiro]], [[Portugal]], the first Anglo-Portuguese victory of the [[Peninsular War]].
*[[1810]] &amp;ndash; [[Charles XIV John of Sweden|Jean-Baptiste Bernadotte]], [[Marshal of France]], is elected [[Crown prince|Crown Prince]] of [[Sweden]] by the Swedish [[Riksdag of the Estates]].
*[[1821]] &amp;ndash; [[Jarvis Island]] is discovered by the crew of the ship, ''Eliza Frances''.
*[[1831]] &amp;ndash; [[Nat Turner]] leads black slaves and free blacks in a rebellion.
*[[1852]] &amp;ndash; [[Tlingit people|Tlingit Indians]] destroy [[Fort Selkirk, Yukon]] Territory.
*[[1863]] &amp;ndash; [[Lawrence, Kansas]] is destroyed by [[Confederate States of America|Confederate]] guerrillas [[Quantrill's Raiders]] in the [[Lawrence Massacre]].
*[[1879]] &amp;ndash; The Virgin Mary, along with St. Joseph and St. John the Evangelist, reportedly [[Marian apparition|appears]] at [[Knock Shrine]] in [[Knock, County Mayo]], [[Ireland]].
*[[1883]] &amp;ndash; [[1883 Rochester tornado|An F5 tornado]] strikes [[Rochester, Minnesota]], leading to the creation of the [[Mayo Clinic]].
*[[1888]] &amp;ndash; The first successful [[adding machine]] in the [[United States]] is patented by [[William Seward Burroughs I|William Seward Burroughs]].
*[[1897]] &amp;ndash; [[Oldsmobile]], a brand of American automobiles, is founded.
*[[1911]] &amp;ndash; The ''[[Mona Lisa]]'' is [[art theft|stolen]] by a [[Musée du Louvre|Louvre]] employee.
*[[1914]] &amp;ndash; [[World War I]]: The [[Battle of Charleroi]], a successful German attack across the River [[Sambre]] which pre-empted a French offensive in the same area.
*[[1918]] &amp;ndash; World War I: The [[Second Battle of the Somme (1918)|Second Battle of the Somme]] begins.
*[[1942]] &amp;ndash; [[World War II]]: The flag of [[Nazi Germany]] is installed atop the [[Mount Elbrus]], the highest peak of the [[Caucasus Mountains|Caucasus]] mountain range.
*  1942   &amp;ndash; World War II: The [[Guadalcanal Campaign]]: American forces defeat an attack by [[Imperial Japanese Army]] soldiers in the [[Battle of the Tenaru]].
*[[1944]] &amp;ndash; [[Dumbarton Oaks Conference]], prelude to the [[United Nations]], begins.
*  1944   &amp;ndash; World War II: [[Canada|Canadian]] and [[Poland|Polish]] units [[Operation Tractable|capture]] the strategically important town of [[Falaise, Calvados]], [[France]].
*[[1945]] &amp;ndash; Physicist [[Harry K. Daghlian, Jr.]] is fatally irradiated in a [[criticality accident]] during an experiment with the [[Demon core]] at [[Los Alamos National Laboratory]].
*[[1957]] &amp;ndash; The [[Soviet Union]] successfully conducts a long-range test flight of the [[R-7 Semyorka]], the first [[intercontinental ballistic missile]].
*[[1959]] &amp;ndash; [[President of the United States|United States President]] [[Dwight D. Eisenhower]] signs an executive order proclaiming [[Hawaii]] the 50th state of the union. Hawaii's admission is currently commemorated by [[Hawaii Admission Day]]
*[[1961]] &amp;ndash; [[Motown]] releases what would be its first #1 hit, &quot;[[Please Mr. Postman]]&quot; by [[The Marvelettes]].
*[[1963]] &amp;ndash; [[Xá Lợi Pagoda raids]]: The [[Army of the Republic of Vietnam Special Forces]] loyal to [[Ngo Dinh Nhu]], brother of President [[Ngo Dinh Diem]], vandalizes [[Buddhism|Buddhist]] pagodas across the country, arresting thousands and leaving an estimated hundreds dead.
*[[1968]] &amp;ndash; [[Nicolae Ceaușescu]], leader of [[Communist Romania]], publicly condemns the [[Soviet Union|Soviet]] led [[Warsaw Pact invasion of Czechoslovakia]], encouraging the [[Romanians|Romanian]] population to arm itself against possible Soviet reprisals.
*  1968   &amp;ndash; [[James Anderson, Jr.]] posthumously receives the first [[Medal of Honor]] to be awarded to an [[African American]] [[United States Marine Corps|U.S. Marine]].
*[[1969]] &amp;ndash; An [[Australia]]n, [[Denis Michael Rohan]], sets the [[Al-Aqsa Mosque]] on fire, a major catalyst of the formation of the [[Organisation of Islamic Cooperation]].
*[[1971]] &amp;ndash; A bomb exploded in the [[Liberal Party (Philippines)|Liberal Party]] campaign rally in [[Plaza Miranda bombing|Plaza Miranda]], [[Manila]], [[Philippines]] with several anti-[[Ferdinand Marcos|Marcos]] political candidates injured.
*[[1976]] &amp;ndash; [[Axe murder incident#Operation Paul Bunyan|Operation Paul Bunyan]] at [[Panmunjom]], [[South Korea]].
*[[1979]] &amp;ndash; Soviet dancer [[Alexander Godunov]] defects to the United States.
*[[1982]] &amp;ndash; [[Lebanese Civil War]]: The first troops of a [[Multinational Force in Lebanon|multinational force]] lands in [[Beirut]] to oversee the [[Palestine Liberation Organization]]'s withdrawal from [[Lebanon]].
*[[1983]] &amp;ndash; Philippine opposition leader [[Benigno Aquino, Jr.]] is assassinated at the Manila International Airport (now renamed ''[[Ninoy Aquino International Airport]]'').
*[[1986]] &amp;ndash; Carbon dioxide gas erupts from [[volcano|volcanic]] [[Lake Nyos]] in [[Cameroon]], killing up to 1,800 people within a 20-kilometer range.
*[[1991]] &amp;ndash; [[Latvia]] declares renewal of its full independence after the occupation of Soviet Union.
*  1991   &amp;ndash; [[1991 Soviet coup d'état attempt|Coup attempt]] against [[Mikhail Gorbachev]] collapses.
*[[1992]] &amp;ndash; [[Ruby Ridge]] Standoff in [[Idaho]]
*[[1993]] &amp;ndash; [[NASA]] loses contact with the [[Mars Observer]] spacecraft.
*[[2001]] &amp;ndash; [[NATO]] decides to send a peace-keeping force to the former [[Socialist Federal Republic of Yugoslavia|Yugoslav]] [[Republic of Macedonia]].
*  2001   &amp;ndash; The [[International Red Cross and Red Crescent Movement|Red Cross]] announces that a [[famine]] is striking [[Tajikistan]], and calls for international financial aid for Tajikistan and [[Uzbekistan]].
*[[2013]] &amp;ndash; Hundreds of people are reported killed by [[2013 Ghouta attacks|chemical attacks]] in the [[Ghouta]] region of [[Syria]].
