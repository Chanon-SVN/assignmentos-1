==Events==
*[[1457 BC]] &amp;ndash; Likely date of the [[Battle of Megiddo (15th century BC)|Battle of Megiddo]] between [[Thutmose III]] and a large [[Canaan]]ite coalition under the King of [[Kadesh]], the first battle to have been recorded in what is accepted as relatively reliable detail.
*  [[73]] &amp;ndash; [[Masada]], a [[Judaism|Jewish]] fortress, falls to the [[Roman Empire|Romans]] after several months of siege, ending the [[First Jewish–Roman War|Great Jewish Revolt]].
*[[1346]] &amp;ndash; [[Dušan the Mighty]] is proclaimed Emperor, with the [[Serbian Empire]] occupying much of the [[Balkans]].
*[[1520]] &amp;ndash; The [[Revolt of the Comuneros]] begins in [[Spain]] against the rule of [[Charles V, Holy Roman Emperor|Charles V]].
*[[1521]] &amp;ndash; [[Protestant Reformation]]: [[Martin Luther]]'s first appearance before the [[Diet of Worms]] to be examined by the [[Charles V, Holy Roman Emperor|Holy Roman Emperor Charles V]] and the other estates of the [[empire]].
*[[1582]] &amp;ndash; [[Spain|Spanish]] [[conquistador]] [[Hernando de Lerma]] founds the settlement of [[Salta]], [[Argentina]].
*[[1746]] &amp;ndash; The [[Battle of Culloden]] is fought between the French-supported [[Jacobitism|Jacobites]] and the British [[House of Hanover|Hanoverian]] forces commanded by [[Prince William, Duke of Cumberland|William Augustus, Duke of Cumberland]], in Scotland.  After the battle many highland traditions were banned and the Highlands of Scotland were cleared of inhabitants.
*[[1780]] &amp;ndash; The [[University of Münster]] in [[Münster]], [[North Rhine-Westphalia]], [[Germany]] is founded.
*[[1799]] &amp;ndash; [[Napoleonic Wars]]: The [[Battle of Mount Tabor]] &amp;ndash; [[Napoleon I of France|Napoleon]] drives [[Ottoman Turks]] across the [[River Jordan]] near [[Acre, Israel|Acre]].
*[[1818]] &amp;ndash; The [[United States Senate]] ratifies the [[Rush-Bagot Treaty]], establishing the border with [[Canada]].
*[[1847]] &amp;ndash; The accidental shooting of a [[Māori people|Māori]] by an [[England|English]] sailor results in the opening of the [[Wanganui Campaign]] of the [[New Zealand land wars]].
*[[1853]] &amp;ndash; The first passenger rail opens in [[India]], from [[Bori Bunder]], [[Bombay]] to [[Thane]].
*[[1858]] &amp;ndash; The [[Wernerian Natural History Society]], a former [[Scotland|Scottish]] [[learned society]], is wound up.
*[[1862]] &amp;ndash; [[American Civil War]]: [[Battle at Lee's Mills]] in [[Virginia]].
*  1862   &amp;ndash; American Civil War: The [[District of Columbia Compensated Emancipation Act]], a bill ending [[slavery in the United States|slavery]] in the [[District of Columbia]], becomes law.
*[[1863]] &amp;ndash; American Civil War: [[Siege of Vicksburg]] &amp;ndash; ships led by [[Union (American Civil War)|Union]] [[Admiral]] [[David Dixon Porter]] move through heavy [[Confederate States of America|Confederate]] artillery fire on approach to [[Vicksburg, Mississippi|Vicksburg]], Mississippi.
*[[1881]] &amp;ndash; In [[Dodge City, Kansas]], [[Bat Masterson]] fights his last gun battle.
*[[1908]] &amp;ndash; [[Natural Bridges National Monument]] is established in [[Utah]].
*[[1910]] &amp;ndash; The oldest existing indoor [[ice hockey]] arena still used for the sport in the 21st century, [[Matthews Arena|Boston Arena]], opens for the first time. 
*[[1912]] &amp;ndash; [[Harriet Quimby]] becomes the first woman to fly an [[Aircraft|airplane]] across the [[English Channel]].
*[[1917]] &amp;ndash; [[Vladimir Lenin]] returns to [[Saint Petersburg|Petrograd]], [[Russian Provisional Government|Russia]] from exile in [[Switzerland]].
*[[1919]] &amp;ndash; [[Mahatma Gandhi|Mohandas Gandhi]] organizes a day of &quot;prayer and fasting&quot; in response to the killing of Indian protesters in the [[Jallianwala Bagh massacre]] by the [[United Kingdom|British]] colonial troops three days earlier.
*  1919   &amp;ndash; [[Polish–Soviet War]]: The [[Polish Land Forces|Polish army]] launches the [[Vilna offensive]] to capture [[Vilnius]] in modern [[Lithuania]].
*[[1922]] &amp;ndash; The [[Treaty of Rapallo, 1922|Treaty of Rapallo]], pursuant to which [[Weimar Republic|Germany]] and the [[Soviet Union]] re-establish [[diplomatic relations]], is signed.
*[[1925]] &amp;ndash; During the [[Communist]] [[St Nedelya Church assault]] in [[Sofia]], [[Kingdom of Bulgaria|Bulgaria]], 150 are killed and 500 are wounded.
*[[1941]] &amp;ndash; [[World War II]]: The Italian convoy ''Duisburg'', directed to [[Tunisia]], is attacked and destroyed by [[United Kingdom|British]] ships.
*  1941   &amp;ndash; World War II: The [[Ustaše]], a [[Croatia]]n [[far-right]] organization is put in charge of the [[Independent State of Croatia]] by the [[Axis Powers]] after the Axis [[Invasion of Yugoslavia|Operation 25]] invasion.
*  1941   &amp;ndash; [[Bob Feller]] of the [[Cleveland Indians]] throws the only [[Opening Day]] [[no-hitter]] in the history of [[Major League Baseball]], beating the [[Chicago White Sox]] 1–0.
*[[1944]] &amp;ndash; World War II: Allied forces start bombing [[Belgrade]], killing about 1,100 people. This bombing fell on the Orthodox Christian Easter.
*[[1945]] &amp;ndash; World War II: The [[Red Army]] begins the [[Battle of Berlin|final assault]] on [[Nazi Germany|German]] forces around [[Berlin]], with nearly one million troops fighting in the [[Battle of the Seelow Heights]].
*  1945   &amp;ndash; The [[United States Army]] liberates Nazi ''Sonderlager'' (high security) [[prisoner-of-war camp]] [[Oflag IV-C]] (better known as [[Colditz Castle|Colditz]]).
*  1945   &amp;ndash; More than 7,000 die when the German refugee ship ''[[Goya (ship)|Goya]]'' is sunk by a Soviet [[submarine]].
*[[1947]] &amp;ndash; [[Texas City Disaster]]: An explosion on board a freighter in port causes the city of [[Texas City, Texas]], to catch fire, killing almost 600.
*  1947   &amp;ndash; [[Bernard Baruch]] coins the term &quot;[[Cold War]]&quot; to describe the relationship between the [[United States]] and the [[Soviet Union]].
*[[1953]] &amp;ndash; [[Elizabeth II of the United Kingdom|Queen Elizabeth II]] launches the [[Royal Yacht]] {{Ship|HMY|Britannia}}.
*[[1961]] &amp;ndash; In a nationally broadcast speech, [[Cuba]]n leader [[Fidel Castro]] declares that he is a [[Marxism–Leninism|Marxist–Leninist]] and that Cuba is going to adopt [[Communism]].
*[[1962]] &amp;ndash; [[Walter Cronkite]] takes over as the lead [[news anchor]] of the ''[[CBS Evening News]]'', during which time he would become &quot;the most trusted man in America&quot;.
*[[1963]] &amp;ndash; [[Martin Luther King, Jr.|Dr. Martin Luther King, Jr.]] pens his [[Letter from Birmingham Jail]] while incarcerated in [[Birmingham, Alabama]] for protesting against segregation.
*[[1972]] &amp;ndash; [[Apollo program]]: The launch of [[Apollo 16]] from [[Cape Canaveral, Florida]].
*[[1990]] &amp;ndash; The &quot;Doctor of Death&quot;, [[Jack Kevorkian]], participates in his first [[assisted suicide]].
*[[1992]] &amp;ndash; The ''[[Katina P]]'' runs aground off of [[Maputo]], [[Mozambique]] and 60,000 tons of crude oil spill into the ocean.
*[[2001]] &amp;ndash; [[India]] and [[Bangladesh]] begin a [[2001 Indian–Bangladeshi border conflict|five-day border conflict]], but are unable to resolve the disputes about [[India–Bangladesh border|their border]].
*[[2003]] &amp;ndash; The [[Treaty of Accession 2003|Treaty of Accession]] is signed in [[Athens]] admitting ten new member states to the [[European Union]].
*[[2007]] &amp;ndash; [[Virginia Tech massacre]]: [[Seung-Hui Cho]] guns down 32 people and injures 17 before committing suicide.
*[[2012]] &amp;ndash; The [[Trial of Anders Behring Breivik|trial]] for [[Anders Behring Breivik]], the perpetrator of the [[2011 Norway attacks]], begins in [[Oslo]], [[Norway]].
*  2012   &amp;ndash; The [[2012 Pulitzer Prize|Pulitzer Prize]] winners were announced, it was the first time [[1977 Pulitzer Prize|since 1977]] that no book won the [[Pulitzer Prize for Fiction|Fiction Prize]].
*[[2013]] &amp;ndash; A 7.8-[[Moment magnitude scale|magnitude]] [[2013 Sistan and Baluchestan earthquake|earthquake]] strikes [[Sistan and Baluchestan Province]], [[Iran]], killing at least 35 people and injuring 117 others.
*[[2014]] &amp;ndash; The [[MV Sewol|MV ''Sewol'']] ferry carrying more than 450 people [[Sinking of the MV Sewol|capsizes]] near [[Jindo (island)|Jindo Island]] off [[South Korea]], leaving 295 passengers and crew dead and 9 more missing.
