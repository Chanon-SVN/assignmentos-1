==Events==
*[[30 BC]] &amp;ndash; [[Augustus|Octavian]] (later known as Augustus) enters [[Alexandria]], [[Egypt]], bringing it under the control of the [[Roman Republic]].
*  [[69]] &amp;ndash; [[Revolt of the Batavi|Batavian rebellion]]: The [[Batavi (Germanic tribe)|Batavians]] in [[Germania Inferior]] (Netherlands) revolt under the leadership of [[Gaius Julius Civilis]].
* [[527]] &amp;ndash; [[Justinian I]] becomes the sole ruler of the [[Byzantine Empire]].
* [[607]] &amp;ndash; [[Ono no Imoko]] is dispatched as envoy to the [[Sui Dynasty|Sui]] court in China (Traditional [[Japanese calendar|Japanese date]]: July 3, 607).
* [[902]] &amp;ndash; [[Taormina]], the last Byzantine stronghold in [[Sicily]], is captured by the [[Aghlabids]] army, concluding the [[Muslim conquest of Sicily]].
*[[1192]] &amp;ndash; [[Richard I of England|Richard the Lionheart]] landed on [[Jaffa]] and defeated the army of [[Saladin]]
*[[1203]] &amp;ndash; [[Isaac II Angelos]], restored [[List of Byzantine emperors|Eastern Roman Emperor]], declares his son [[Alexios IV Angelos]] co-emperor after pressure from the forces of the [[Fourth Crusade]].
*[[1291]] &amp;ndash; The [[Old Swiss Confederacy]] is formed with the signature of the [[Federal Charter of 1291|Federal Charter]].
*[[1469]] &amp;ndash; [[Louis XI of France]] founds the [[chivalric order]] called the [[Order of Saint Michael]] in [[Amboise]].
*[[1498]] &amp;ndash; [[Christopher Columbus]] becomes the first European to visit what is now [[Venezuela]].
*[[1620]] &amp;ndash; The [[Speedwell (ship)|Speedwell]] leaves [[Delfshaven]] to bring [[Pilgrims (Plymouth Colony)|pilgrims]] to [[Plymouth Colony|America]] by way of [[England]].
*[[1664]] &amp;ndash; [[Ottoman Empire|Ottoman forces]] are defeated in the [[Battle of Saint Gotthard (1664)|battle of Saint Gotthard]] by an [[Habsburg Monarchy|Austrian]] army led by [[Raimondo Montecuccoli]], resulting in the [[Peace of Vasvár]].
*[[1714]] &amp;ndash; George of [[Hanover]] becomes [[George I of Great Britain]], marking the beginning of the [[Georgian era]] of British history.
*[[1715]] &amp;ndash; The [[Riot Act]] comes into force in  [[England]].
*[[1759]] &amp;ndash; [[Seven Years' War]]: The [[Battle of Minden]], an allied Anglo-German army victory over the French. In Britain this was one of a number of events that constituted the [[Annus Mirabilis of 1759]] and is celebrated as [[Minden Day]] by certain [[British Army]] regiments.
*[[1774]] &amp;ndash; British scientist [[Joseph Priestley]] discovers [[oxygen]] gas, corroborating the prior discovery of this element by German-Swedish chemist [[Carl Wilhelm Scheele]].
*[[1798]] &amp;ndash; [[French Revolutionary Wars]]: [[Battle of the Nile|Battle of the Nile (Battle of Aboukir Bay)]] &amp;ndash; Battle begins when a British fleet engages the French Revolutionary Navy fleet in an unusual night action.
*[[1800]] &amp;ndash; The [[Acts of Union 1800]] is passed in which merges the [[Kingdom of Great Britain]] and the [[Kingdom of Ireland]] into the [[United Kingdom of Great Britain and Ireland]].
*[[1801]] &amp;ndash; [[First Barbary War]]: The American [[schooner]] {{USS|Enterprise|1799|6}} [[Action of 1 August 1801|captures]] the [[Tripoli]]tan [[polacca]] ''Tripoli'' in a [[list of single-ship actions|single-ship action]] off the coast of modern-day [[Libya]].
*[[1831]] &amp;ndash; A new [[London Bridge]] opens.
*[[1834]] &amp;ndash; [[Slavery in the British Isles|Slavery]] is abolished in the [[British Empire]] as the [[Slavery Abolition Act 1833]] comes into force.
*[[1838]] &amp;ndash; Non-laborer slaves in most of the British Empire are emancipated.
*[[1840]] &amp;ndash; Laborer slaves in most of the British Empire are emancipated.
*[[1842]] &amp;ndash; The [[Lombard Street Riot]] erupts in [[Philadelphia]], [[Pennsylvania]], [[United States]].
*[[1855]] &amp;ndash; The first ascent of [[Monte Rosa]], the second highest summit in the Alps.
*[[1876]] &amp;ndash; [[Colorado]] is admitted as the 38th [[U.S. state]].
*[[1894]] &amp;ndash; The [[First Sino-Japanese War]] erupts between [[Empire of Japan|Japan]] and [[Qing Dynasty|China]] over [[Korea]].
*[[1907]] &amp;ndash; The start of the [[Brownsea Island Scout camp|first Scout camp]] on [[Brownsea Island]], the origin of the worldwide [[Scouting]] movement.
*[[1914]] &amp;ndash; The [[German Empire]] declares war on the [[Russian Empire]] at the opening of [[World War I]]. The [[Military of Switzerland|Swiss Army]] mobilizes because of World War I.
*[[1927]] &amp;ndash; The [[Nanchang Uprising]] marks the first significant battle in the [[Chinese Civil War]] between the [[Kuomintang]] and [[Communist Party of China|Chinese Communist Party]]. This day is commemorated as the anniversary of the founding of the [[People's Liberation Army]].
*[[1937]] &amp;ndash; [[Josip Broz Tito]] reads the resolution &quot;Manifesto of constitutional congress of KPH&quot; to the constitutive congress of KPH ([[League of Communists of Croatia|Croatian Communist Party]]) in woods near [[Samobor]].
*[[1944]] &amp;ndash; [[World War II]]: the [[Warsaw Uprising]] against the [[Nazism|Nazi]] occupation breaks out in [[Warsaw]], [[Poland]].
*[[1946]] &amp;ndash; Leaders of the [[Russian Liberation Army]], a force of Russian prisoners of war that [[Collaboration with the Axis Powers during World War II|collaborated with Nazi Germany]], are executed in [[Moscow]], [[Soviet Union]] for treason.
*[[1957]] &amp;ndash; The United States and [[Canada]] form the [[North American Aerospace Defense Command]] (NORAD).
*[[1960]] &amp;ndash; [[Dahomey]] (later renamed [[Benin]]) declares independence from France.
*  1960   &amp;ndash; [[Islamabad]] is declared the [[federal capital]] of the [[Government of Pakistan]].
*[[1961]] &amp;ndash; U.S. Defense Secretary [[Robert McNamara]] orders the creation of the [[Defense Intelligence Agency]] (DIA), the nation's first centralized military espionage organization.
*[[1964]] &amp;ndash; The former [[Belgian Congo]] is renamed the [[Democratic Republic of the Congo]].
*[[1966]] &amp;ndash; [[Charles Whitman]] kills 16 people at the [[University of Texas at Austin]] before being killed by the police.
*  1966   &amp;ndash; Purges of intellectuals and imperialists becomes official China policy at the beginning of the [[Cultural Revolution]].
*[[1968]] &amp;ndash; The coronation is held of [[Hassanal Bolkiah]], the 29th [[List of Sultans of Brunei|Sultan of Brunei]].
*[[1974]] &amp;ndash; [[Cyprus dispute]]: The [[United Nations Security Council]] authorizes the [[United Nations Peacekeeping Force in Cyprus|UNFICYP]] to create the &quot;[[United Nations Buffer Zone in Cyprus|Green Line]]&quot;, dividing [[Cyprus]] into two zones.
*[[1975]] &amp;ndash; [[Helsinki Accords|CSCE Final Act]] creates the [[Organization for Security and Co-operation in Europe|Conference for Security and Co-operation in Europe]].
*[[1980]] &amp;ndash; A [[Buttevant Rail Disaster|train crash]] kills 18 people in [[County Cork]], [[Republic of Ireland|Ireland]].
*  1980   &amp;ndash; [[Vigdís Finnbogadóttir]] is elected [[President of Iceland]] and becomes the world's first democratically elected female head of state.
*  1980   &amp;ndash; [[Patrick Depailler]], French Grand Prix driver was killed in a crash at [[Hockenheim]] during a private test session.
*[[1981]] &amp;ndash; [[MTV]] begins broadcasting in the United States and airs its first video, &quot;[[Video Killed the Radio Star]]&quot; by [[The Buggles]].
*[[1984]] &amp;ndash; Commercial [[peat]]-cutters discover the preserved [[bog body]] of a man, called [[Lindow Man]], at [[Lindow Moss]], [[Cheshire]], northwest England
*[[1993]] &amp;ndash; The [[Great Mississippi and Missouri Rivers Flood of 1993]] comes to a peak.
*[[2001]] &amp;ndash; [[Alabama Supreme Court]] [[Chief Justice]] [[Roy Moore]] has a [[Ten Commandments]] monument installed in the judiciary building, leading to a [[Glassroth v. Moore|lawsuit]] to have it removed and his own removal from office.
*[[2004]] &amp;ndash; A [[Ycuá Bolaños supermarket fire|supermarket fire]] kills 396 people and injures 500 others in [[Asunción]], [[Paraguay]].
*[[2007]] &amp;ndash; The [[I-35W Mississippi River bridge]] spanning the [[Mississippi River]] in [[Minneapolis|Minneapolis, Minnesota]], collapses during the evening [[rush hour]].
*[[2008]] &amp;ndash; Eleven mountaineers from international expeditions died on K2, the second-highest mountain on Earth in [[2008 K2 disaster|the worst single accident in the history of K2 mountaineering]].
