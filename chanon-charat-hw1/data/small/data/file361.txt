==Events==
* [[769]] &amp;ndash; The [[Lateran Council (769)|Lateran Council]] condemned the [[Council of Hieria]] and anathematized its [[Iconoclasm|iconoclastic]] rulings.
*[[1071]] &amp;ndash; [[Bari]], the last [[Byzantine Empire|Byzantine]] possession in southern Italy, [[siege of Bari|is surrendered]] to [[Robert Guiscard]].
*[[1395]] &amp;ndash; [[Tokhtamysh–Timur war]]: [[Battle of the Terek River]]: [[Timur]] defeats [[Tokhtamysh]] of the [[Golden Horde]] at the [[Volga]]. The Golden Horde capital city, [[Sarai (city)|Sarai]], is razed to the ground and Timur installs a puppet ruler on the Golden Horde throne. Tokhtamysh escapes to [[Grand Duchy of Lithuania|Lithuania]].
*[[1450]] &amp;ndash; [[Battle of Formigny]]: Toward the end of the [[Hundred Years' War]], the French attack and nearly annihilate English forces, ending English domination in Northern France.
*[[1632]] &amp;ndash; [[Battle of Rain]]: [[Sweden|Swedes]] under [[Gustavus Adolphus of Sweden|Gustavus Adolphus]] defeat the [[Holy Roman Empire]] during the [[Thirty Years' War]].
*[[1638]] &amp;ndash; [[Tokugawa shogunate]] forces put down the [[Shimabara Rebellion]] when they retake [[Hara Castle]] from the rebels.
*[[1642]] &amp;ndash; [[Irish Confederate Wars]]: A [[Confederate Ireland|Confederate Irish]] militia is routed in the [[Battle of Kilrush]] when it attempts to halt the progress of a [[Roundhead|Parliamentarian army]].
*[[1715]] &amp;ndash; The [[Yamasee War#Pocotaligo massacre|Pocotaligo Massacre]] triggers the start of the [[Yamasee War]] in [[Province of Carolina|colonial South Carolina]].
*[[1738]] &amp;ndash; ''[[Serse]]'', an [[Italian opera]] by [[George Frideric Handel]] receives its premiere performance in [[London]], England.
*[[1755]] &amp;ndash; [[Samuel Johnson]]'s ''[[A Dictionary of the English Language]]'' is published in London.
*[[1783]] &amp;ndash; Preliminary articles of peace ending the [[American Revolutionary War]] (or American War of Independence) are ratified.
*[[1802]] &amp;ndash; [[William Wordsworth]] and his sister, [[Dorothy Wordsworth|Dorothy]] see a &quot;long belt&quot; of [[daffodil]]s, inspiring the former to pen ''[[I Wandered Lonely as a Cloud]]''.
*[[1817]] &amp;ndash; [[Thomas Hopkins Gallaudet]] and [[Laurent Clerc]] founded the [[American School for the Deaf]], the first American school for [[deaf]] students, in [[Hartford, Connecticut]].
*[[1861]] &amp;ndash; President [[Abraham Lincoln]] calls for [[President Lincoln's 75,000 Volunteers|75,000 Volunteers]] to quell the insurrection that soon became the [[American Civil War]]
*[[1865]] &amp;ndash; [[Abraham Lincoln]] dies after being shot the previous evening by actor [[John Wilkes Booth]].
*[[1892]] &amp;ndash; The [[General Electric|General Electric Company]] is formed. [[Image:Titanic-New York Herald front page.jpeg|right|thumb|{{RMS|Titanic}} disaster.]]
*[[1896]] &amp;ndash; Closing ceremony of the [[1896 Summer Olympics|Games of the I Olympiad]] in [[Athens]], [[Greece]].
*[[1900]] &amp;ndash; [[Philippine–American War]]: [[Filipinos|Filipino]] guerrillas launch a surprise attack on U.S. infantry and begin a [[Siege of Catubig|four-day siege]] of [[Catubig, Northern Samar|Catubig]], [[Philippines]].
*[[1907]] &amp;ndash; [[Triangle Fraternity]] was founded at the [[University of Illinois at Urbana–Champaign]]
*[[1912]] &amp;ndash; The British passenger liner {{RMS|Titanic}} sinks in the [[Atlantic Ocean|North Atlantic]] at 2:20 a.m., two hours and forty minutes after hitting an [[iceberg]]. Only 710 of 2,227 passengers and crew on board survive.
*[[1920]] &amp;ndash; Two security guards are murdered during a robbery in [[South Braintree, Massachusetts]]. [[Anarchism|Anarchists]] [[Sacco and Vanzetti]] would be convicted of and executed for the crime, amid much controversy.
*[[1921]] &amp;ndash; [[Black Friday (1921)|Black Friday]]: mine owners announce more wage and price cuts, leading to the threat of a strike all across England.&lt;!-- this doesn't sound right --&gt;
*[[1922]] &amp;ndash; U.S. Senator [[John B. Kendrick]] of [[Wyoming]] introduces a resolution calling for an investigation of secret land deal, which leads to the discovery of the [[Teapot Dome scandal]].
*[[1923]] &amp;ndash; [[Insulin]] becomes generally available for use by people with [[diabetes mellitus|diabetes]].
*[[1924]] &amp;ndash; [[Rand McNally]] publishes its first [[road atlas]].
*[[1927]] &amp;ndash; The [[Great Mississippi Flood of 1927]], the most destructive river [[flood]] in U.S. history, begins.
*[[1935]] &amp;ndash; [[Roerich Pact]] signed in [[Washington, D.C.]]
*[[1936]] &amp;ndash; First day of the [[1936–39 Arab revolt in Palestine|Arab revolt]] in [[Mandatory Palestine]].
*  1936   &amp;ndash; [[Aer Lingus]] (Aer Loingeas) is founded by the Irish government as the national airline of the [[Republic of Ireland]].
*[[1940]] &amp;ndash; The [[Allies of World War II|Allies]] begin their attack on the [[Norway|Norwegian]] town of [[Narvik]] which is occupied by [[Nazi Germany]].
*[[1941]] &amp;ndash; In the [[Belfast Blitz]], two-hundred bombers of the German [[Luftwaffe]] attack [[Belfast]], [[Northern Ireland]], [[United Kingdom]] killing one thousand people.
*[[1942]] &amp;ndash; The [[George Cross]] is awarded &quot;to the island fortress of [[Malta]] &amp;ndash; its people and defenders&quot; by [[George VI of the United Kingdom|King George VI]].
*[[1945]] &amp;ndash; The [[Bergen-Belsen concentration camp]] is liberated.
*[[1947]] &amp;ndash; [[Jackie Robinson]] debuts for the [[History of the Brooklyn Dodgers|Brooklyn Dodgers]], breaking [[Baseball color line|baseball's color line]].
*[[1952]] &amp;ndash; The maiden flight of the [[B-52 Stratofortress]]
*[[1955]] &amp;ndash; [[McDonald's]] restaurant dates its founding to the opening of a franchised restaurant by [[Ray Kroc]], in [[Des Plaines, Illinois]]
*[[1957]] &amp;ndash; [[White Rock, British Columbia]] officially separates from [[Surrey, British Columbia]] and is [[municipal incorporation|incorporated]] as a new city.
*[[1960]] &amp;ndash; At [[Shaw University]] in [[Raleigh, North Carolina]], [[Ella Baker]] leads a conference that results in the creation of the [[Student Nonviolent Coordinating Committee]], one of the principal organizations of the [[African-American Civil Rights Movement (1955–1968)|African-American Civil Rights Movement]] in the 1960s.
*[[1964]] &amp;ndash; The first [[Ford Mustang]] rolls off the show room floor, two days before it is set to go on sale nationwide.
*[[1969]] &amp;ndash; The [[EC-121 shootdown incident]]: [[North Korea]] shoots down a [[United States Navy]] aircraft over the [[Sea of Japan]], killing all 31 on board.
*[[1970]] &amp;ndash; During the [[Cambodian Civil War]], massacres of the [[Vietnamese Cambodian|Vietnamese minority]] results in 800 bodies flowing down the [[Mekong River]] into [[South Vietnam]].
*[[1984]] &amp;ndash; The inaugural [[World Youth Day]] is held in [[St. Peter's Square]], Vatican City.
*[[1986]] &amp;ndash; The [[United States]] launches [[Operation El Dorado Canyon]], its bombing raids against [[History of Libya under Muammar Gaddafi#Great Socialist People's Libyan Arab Jamahiriya (1977–2012)|Libyan targets]] in response to a bombing in West Germany that killed two U.S. servicemen.
*[[1989]] &amp;ndash; [[Hillsborough disaster]]: A human crush occurs at [[Hillsborough Stadium]], home of [[Sheffield Wednesday F.C.|Sheffield Wednesday]], in the FA Cup Semi-final, resulting in the deaths of 96 [[Liverpool F.C.|Liverpool]] fans.
*  1989   &amp;ndash; Upon [[Hu Yaobang]]'s death, the [[Tiananmen Square protests of 1989]] begin in [[China]].
*[[2013]] &amp;ndash; [[Boston Marathon bombings|Two bombs explode]] near the finish line at the [[2013 Boston Marathon|Boston Marathon]] in [[Boston, Massachusetts]], killing three people and injuring 264 others.
*[[2014]] &amp;ndash; More than 200 female students are declared missing after a [[2014 Chibok kidnapping|mass kidnapping]] in [[Borno State]], [[Nigeria]].
*  2014   &amp;ndash; A total [[April 2014 lunar eclipse|lunar eclipse]] occurs, producing a Blood Moon.
