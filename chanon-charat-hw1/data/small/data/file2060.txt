==Languages==
[[Bosnian language|Bosnian]], [[Serbian language|Serbian]], and [[Croatian language|Croatian]] are co-official standards of a single language (generally called [[Serbo-Croatian]] in English) and as such are mutually intelligible among all three ethnic groups. All speak the [[Ijekavian accent]] of the [[Shtokavian dialect]]. Serbian is written in both Latin and [[Serbian Cyrillic alphabet|Cyrillic]], whereas Croatian and Bosnian is written only in a [[Gaj's Latin alphabet|Latin alphabet]]. There are also minor speakers of Italian, German, Turkish and Ladino.  [[Yugoslav Sign Language]] is used with Croatian and Serbian variants.
