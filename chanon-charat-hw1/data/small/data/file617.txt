==Events==
* [[711]] &amp;ndash; [[Umayyad conquest of Hispania|Islamic conquest of Hispania]]: [[Moors|Moorish]] troops led by [[Tariq ibn-Ziyad]] land at [[Gibraltar]] to begin their [[invasion]] of the [[Iberian Peninsula]] ([[Al-Andalus]]).
*[[1091]] &amp;ndash; [[Battle of Levounion]]: The [[Pechenegs]] are defeated by Byzantine [[Emperor Alexius I]].
*[[1386]] &amp;ndash; [[Battle of the Vikhra River]]: The [[Principality of Smolensk]] is defeated by the [[Grand Duchy of Lithuania]] and becomes its vassal.
*[[1429]] &amp;ndash; [[Joan of Arc]] arrives to relieve the [[Siege of Orleans]].
*[[1483]] &amp;ndash; [[Gran Canaria]], the main island of the [[Canary Islands]] is conquered by the [[Kingdom of Castile]].
*[[1521]] &amp;ndash; [[Swedish War of Liberation]]: Swedish troops under [[Gustav Vasa]] defeat a Danish force under [[Didrik Slagheck]] in the [[Battle of Västerås]] and soon capture the city of Västerås. The Danish-held castle, however, does not surrender to the Swedes until 31 January the following year, after a nine-month siege.
*[[1770]] &amp;ndash; [[James Cook]] arrives at and names [[Botany Bay]], [[Australia]].
*[[1781]] &amp;ndash; [[American Revolutionary War]]: [[Kingdom of Great Britain|British]] and [[Kingdom of France|French]] ships clash in the [[Battle of Fort Royal]] off the coast of [[Martinique]].
*[[1832]] &amp;ndash; [[Évariste Galois]] is released from prison.
*[[1861]] &amp;ndash; [[American Civil War]]: [[Maryland]]'s [[Maryland House of Delegates|House of Delegates]] votes not to secede from the [[Union (American Civil War)|Union]].
*[[1862]] &amp;ndash; American Civil War: [[New Orleans, Louisiana]] falls to Union forces under [[Admiral]] [[David Farragut]].
*[[1864]] &amp;ndash; [[Theta Xi]] fraternity is founded at [[Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute]], the only fraternity to be founded during the [[American Civil War]].
*[[1882]] &amp;ndash; The &quot;[[Elektromote]]&quot; &amp;ndash; forerunner of the [[trolleybus]] &amp;ndash; is tested by [[Ernst Werner von Siemens]] in [[Berlin]].
*[[1903]] &amp;ndash; A 30 million cubic-metre [[Frank Slide|landslide]] kills 70 in [[Frank, Alberta|Frank, North-West Territories]], [[Canada]].
*[[1910]] &amp;ndash; The [[Parliament of the United Kingdom]] passes the [[People's Budget]], the first budget in British history with the expressed intent of [[Redistribution of wealth|redistributing wealth]] among the British public.
*[[1916]] &amp;ndash; [[World War I]]: The [[United Kingdom|British]] [[6th Indian Division]] surrenders to [[Ottoman Empire|Ottoman Forces]] at the [[Siege of Kut]] in one of the largest surrenders of British forces up to that point.
*  1916   &amp;ndash; [[Easter Rising]]: [[Martial law]] in [[Ireland]] is lifted and the [[rebellion]] is officially over with the surrender of [[Irish nationalism|Irish nationalists]] to [[United Kingdom of Great Britain and Ireland|British]] authorities in [[Dublin]].
*[[1944]] &amp;ndash; [[World War II]]: British agent [[Nancy Wake]], a leading figure in the [[French Resistance]] and the [[Gestapo]]'s most wanted person, parachutes back into France to become a liaison between London and the local [[maquis (World War II)|maquis]] group.
*[[1945]] &amp;ndash; World War II: The [[Nazi Germany|German]] army in [[Italy]] unconditionally surrenders to the [[Allies of World War II|Allies]].
*  1945   &amp;ndash; World War II: Start of [[Operations Manna and Chowhound|Operation Manna]].
*  1945   &amp;ndash; World War II: The [[Captain class frigate]] HMS Goodall K479 is torpedoed by U-286 outside the Kola Inlet becoming the last ship of the Royal Navy sunk in the European theatre of World War II.
*  1945   &amp;ndash; World War II &amp;ndash; [[Fuehrerbunker]]: [[Adolf Hitler]] marries his longtime partner [[Eva Braun]] in a [[Berlin]] bunker and designates [[Admiral]] [[Karl Dönitz]] as his successor. Both Hitler and Braun commit [[suicide]] the following day.
*  1945   &amp;ndash; The [[Dachau concentration camp]] is liberated by [[United States]] troops.
*  1945   &amp;ndash; The [[Italy|Italian]] commune of [[Fornovo di Taro]] is liberated from [[Germany|German]] forces by [[Brazilian Expeditionary Force (FEB)|Brazilian]] forces.
*[[1946]] &amp;ndash; The [[International Military Tribunal for the Far East]] convenes and indicts former [[Prime Minister of Japan]] [[Hideki Tojo]] and 28 former [[Japan]]ese leaders for [[war crime]]s.
*  1946   &amp;ndash; [[Father Divine]], a controversial religious leader who claims to be [[God]], marries the much-younger [[Edna Rose Ritchings]], a celebrated anniversary in the [[International Peace Mission movement]].
*[[1951]] &amp;ndash; [[Tibet]]an delegates to the [[Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China|Central People's Government]] arrive in [[Beijing]] and draft a [[Seventeen Point Agreement for the Peaceful Liberation of Tibet|Seventeen Point Agreement]] for Chinese sovereignty and Tibetan autonomy.
*[[1953]] &amp;ndash; The first U.S. experimental [[3D television]] broadcast showed an episode of [[Space Patrol (1950 TV series)|Space Patrol]] on [[Los Angeles]] [[American Broadcasting Company|ABC]] affiliate [[KECA-TV]].
*[[1965]] &amp;ndash; [[Pakistan]]'s [[Space and Upper Atmosphere Research Commission]] (SUPARCO) successfully launches its seventh [[rocket]] in its ''Rehber'' series.
*[[1967]] &amp;ndash; After refusing induction into the [[United States Army]] the day before (citing religious reasons), [[Muhammad Ali]] is stripped of his [[boxing]] title.
*[[1968]] &amp;ndash; The controversial musical ''[[Hair (musical)|Hair]]'', a product of the [[hippie]] [[Counterculture of the 1960s|counter-culture]] and [[sexual revolution]] of the 1960s, opens at the [[Biltmore Theatre]] on [[Broadway theatre|Broadway]], with its song becoming anthems of the [[Opposition to United States involvement in the Vietnam War|anti-Vietnam War movement]].
*[[1970]] &amp;ndash; [[Vietnam War]]: United States and [[South Vietnam]]ese forces [[Cambodian Campaign|invade]] [[Cambodia]] to hunt [[National Front for the Liberation of Vietnam|Viet Cong]].
*[[1974]] &amp;ndash; [[Watergate Scandal]]: [[President of the United States|President]] [[Richard Nixon]] announces the release of edited transcripts of [[White House]] tape recordings relating to the scandal.
*[[1975]] &amp;ndash; Vietnam War: [[Operation Frequent Wind]]: The U.S. begins to evacuate U.S. citizens from [[Saigon]] prior to an expected [[North Vietnam]]ese takeover. U.S. involvement in the war comes to an end.
*  1975   &amp;ndash; Vietnam War: The [[North Vietnam]]ese [[People's Army of Vietnam|Army]] completes [[East Sea Campaign|its capture]] of all parts of [[South Vietnam]]ese-held [[Spratly Islands|Trường Sa Islands]].
*[[1986]] &amp;ndash; A fire at the Central library of the City of [[Los Angeles Public Library]] damages or destroys 400,000 books and other items.
*1986 &amp;ndash; [[Chernobyl disaster|The Chernobyl Disaster]]: American and European Spy Satellites capture the ruins of the 4th Reactor at the Chernobyl Power Plant  
*[[1991]] &amp;ndash; A [[1991 Bangladesh cyclone|cyclone]] strikes the [[Chittagong]] district of southeastern [[Bangladesh]] with winds of around {{convert|155|mph|kph}}, killing at least 138,000 people and leaving as many as 10 million homeless.
*[[1992]] &amp;ndash; [[1992 Los Angeles riots|Los Angeles riots]]: [[Riot]]s in Los Angeles, California, following the acquittal of [[police officer]]s charged with excessive force in the beating of [[Rodney King]]. Over the next three days 53 people are killed and hundreds of buildings are destroyed.
*[[1997]] &amp;ndash; The [[Chemical Weapons Convention]] of 1993 enters into force, outlawing the production, stockpiling and use of [[Chemical warfare|chemical weapons]] by its signatories.
*[[1999]] &amp;ndash; The [[Avala TV Tower]] near [[Belgrade]] is destroyed in the [[1999 NATO bombing of the Federal Republic of Yugoslavia|NATO bombing]] of [[Federal Republic of Yugoslavia|Yugoslavia]].
*[[2004]] &amp;ndash; [[Dick Cheney]] and [[George W. Bush]] testify before the [[9/11 Commission]] in a closed, unrecorded hearing in the [[Oval Office]].
*  2004   &amp;ndash; [[Oldsmobile]] builds its final car ending 107 years of production.
*[[2005]] &amp;ndash; [[Syria]] completes withdrawal from [[Lebanon]], ending [[Syrian occupation of Lebanon|29 years of occupation]].
*[[2011]] &amp;ndash; The [[Wedding of Prince William, Duke of Cambridge, and Kate Middleton|Wedding]] of [[Prince William, Duke of Cambridge]] and [[Catherine, Duchess of Cambridge|Kate Middleton]].
*[[2013]] &amp;ndash; A powerful [[2013 Prague explosion|explosion]] occurs in an office building in [[Prague]], [[Czech Republic]], believed to have been caused by natural gas, injures 43 people.
