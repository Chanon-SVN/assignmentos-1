==Events==
* [[392]] &amp;ndash; [[Arbogast (general)|Arbogast]] has [[Eugenius]] elected [[Roman emperor|Western Roman Emperor]].
* [[476]] &amp;ndash; [[Odoacer]] is named ''Rex italiae'' by his troops.
* [[565]] &amp;ndash; [[Columba]] reports seeing a [[Loch Ness Monster|monster]] in [[Loch Ness]], [[Scotland]].
* [[851]] &amp;ndash; [[Battle of Jengland]]: [[Erispoe]] defeats [[Charles the Bald]] near the Breton town of Jengland.
*[[1138]] &amp;ndash; [[Battle of the Standard]] between Scotland and [[England]].
*[[1485]] &amp;ndash; The [[Battle of Bosworth Field]], the death of [[Richard III of England|Richard III]] and the end of the [[House of Plantagenet]].
*[[1559]] &amp;ndash; [[Bartolomé Carranza]], [[Spain|Spanish]] [[archbishop]], is arrested for [[Heresy in Christianity|heresy]].
*[[1639]] &amp;ndash; Madras (now [[Chennai]]), [[India]], is founded by the [[East India Company|British East India Company]] on a sliver of land bought from local Nayak rulers.
*[[1642]] &amp;ndash; [[Charles I of England|Charles I]] calls the [[Parliament of England|English Parliament]] traitors. The [[English Civil War]] begins.
*[[1654]] &amp;ndash; [[Jacob Barsimson]] arrives in [[New Amsterdam]]. He is the first known [[Judaism|Jewish]] [[Immigration|immigrant]] to America.
*[[1711]] &amp;ndash; Ships from British Admiral [[Hovenden Walker]]'s [[Quebec Expedition]] founders on rocks at the mouth of the [[Saint Lawrence River]].
*[[1717]] &amp;ndash; Spanish troops land on [[Sardinia]].
*[[1770]] &amp;ndash; [[James Cook]] names and lands on [[Possession Island (Queensland)|Possession Island]], [[Queensland]] and claims the east coast of [[Australia]] as [[New South Wales]] in the name of [[George III of the United Kingdom|King George III]].
*[[1777]] &amp;ndash; [[American Revolutionary War]]: British forces abandon the [[Siege of Fort Stanwix]] after hearing rumors of [[Continental Army]] reinforcements.
*[[1780]] &amp;ndash; James Cook's ship {{HMS|Resolution|1771|6}} returns to England (Cook having been killed on [[Hawaii]] during the voyage).
*[[1791]] &amp;ndash; Beginning of the [[Haiti]]an [[Haitian Revolution|Slave Revolution]] in [[Saint-Domingue]].
*[[1798]] &amp;ndash; [[France|French]] troops land in [[Kilcummin|Kilcummin harbour]], [[County Mayo]], [[Ireland]] to aid [[Wolfe Tone]]'s [[Society of United Irishmen|United Irishmen's]] [[Irish Rebellion of 1798|Irish Rebellion]].
*[[1827]] &amp;ndash; [[José de la Mar]] becomes [[List of Presidents of Peru|President of Peru]].
*[[1831]] &amp;ndash; [[Nat Turner's slave rebellion]] commences just after midnight in [[Southampton County, Virginia]], leading to the deaths of more than 50 whites and several hundred [[African American]]s who are killed in retaliation for the uprising.
*[[1846]] &amp;ndash; The [[Second Federal Republic of Mexico]] is established.
*[[1848]] &amp;ndash; The [[United States]] annexes [[New Mexico]].
*[[1849]] &amp;ndash; The first [[airstrike|air raid]] in history. Austria launches pilotless balloons against the city of [[Venice]].
*[[1851]] &amp;ndash; The first [[America's Cup]] is won by the [[yacht]] ''[[America (yacht)|America]]''.
*[[1864]] &amp;ndash; Twelve [[nation]]s sign the First [[Geneva Conventions|Geneva Convention]].
*[[1875]] &amp;ndash; The [[Treaty of Saint Petersburg (1875)|Treaty of Saint Petersburg]] between [[Japan]] and [[Russia]] is ratified, providing for the exchange of [[Sakhalin]] for the [[Kuril Islands]].
*[[1902]] &amp;ndash; [[Cadillac]] Motor Company is founded.
*  1902   &amp;ndash; [[Theodore Roosevelt]] becomes the first [[President of the United States]] to ride in an automobile.
*[[1910]] &amp;ndash; [[Korea]] is annexed by [[Japan]] with the signing of the [[Japan–Korea Treaty of 1910]], beginning a period of Japanese rule of Korea that lasted until the end of [[World War II]].
*[[1922]] &amp;ndash; [[Michael Collins (Irish leader)|Michael Collins]], [[Commander-in-chief]] of the [[Irish Free State]] Army, is shot dead during an [[Irish Republican Army (1922–1969)|Anti-Treaty]] ambush at [[Béal na Bláth]], [[County Cork]], during the [[Irish Civil War]].
*[[1932]] &amp;ndash; The [[BBC]] first experiments with television broadcasting. (See also [[Timeline of the BBC#1930s|Timeline of the BBC]].)
*[[1934]] &amp;ndash; [[Bill Woodfull]] of [[Australia national cricket team|Australia]] becomes the only [[cricket]] captain to twice regain [[The Ashes]].
*[[1941]] &amp;ndash; [[World War II]]: German [[troop]]s reach [[Saint Petersburg|Leningrad]], leading to the [[siege of Leningrad]].
*[[1942]] &amp;ndash; World War II: [[Brazil]] [[Vargas Era|declares war]] on Germany and [[Italy]].
*[[1944]] &amp;ndash; World War II: [[Romania]] is captured by the [[Soviet Union]].
*  1944   &amp;ndash; World War II: [[Holocaust of Kedros]] in [[Crete]] by German forces
*[[1949]] &amp;ndash; [[1949 Queen Charlotte Islands earthquake|Queen Charlotte earthquake]]: [[Canada]]'s largest earthquake since the [[1700 Cascadia earthquake]]
*[[1950]] &amp;ndash; [[Althea Gibson]] becomes the first black [[Competition|competitor]] in international [[tennis]].
*[[1952]] &amp;ndash; The [[penal colony]] on [[Devil's Island]] is permanently closed.
*[[1961]] &amp;ndash; [[Ida Siekmann]] dies attempting to cross the [[Berlin Wall]].
*[[1962]] &amp;ndash; An attempt to assassinate French [[president]] [[Charles de Gaulle]] fails.
*[[1963]] &amp;ndash; American [[Joseph A. Walker|Joe Walker]] in an [[North American X-15|X-15]] test plane reaches an altitude of {{convert|106|km|mi|abbr=on}}.
*[[1966]] &amp;ndash; Labor movements [[United Farm Workers|NFWA]] and AWOC merge to become the United Farm Workers Organizing Committee (UFWOC), predecessor of the [[United Farm Workers]].
*[[1968]] &amp;ndash; [[Pope Paul VI]] arrives in [[Bogotá]], [[Colombia]]. It is the first visit of a [[pope]] to [[Latin America]].
*[[1971]] &amp;ndash; [[J. Edgar Hoover]] and [[John N. Mitchell|John Mitchell]] announce the arrest of 20 of [[the Camden 28]].
*[[1972]] &amp;ndash; [[Rhodesia]] is expelled by the [[International Olympic Committee|IOC]] for its racist policies.
*[[1973]] &amp;ndash; The [[Congress of Chile]] votes in favour of a resolution condemning President [[Salvador Allende]]'s government and demands him to resign or else be unseated through force and new elections be called. The first demand is executed eighteen days later in [[1973 Chilean coup d'etat|a bloody coup d'etat]], commencing 17 years of military rule.
*[[1978]] &amp;ndash; The ''[[Sandinista National Liberation Front]]'' (FLSN) occupies national palace in [[Nicaragua]].
*[[1984]] &amp;ndash; [[Murder of Brian Bishop|PC Brian Bishop]] a British police officer is shot in the head by an armed robber in [[Frinton-on-Sea]], [[Essex]]. He dies from his injuries five days later.
*[[1985]] &amp;ndash; [[British Airtours Flight 28M|Manchester Air Disaster]] sees 55 people killed when a [[fire]] breaks out on a commercial aircraft at [[Manchester Airport]].
*[[1989]] &amp;ndash; [[Nolan Ryan]] strikes out [[Rickey Henderson]] to become the first [[Major League Baseball]] [[pitcher]] to record 5,000 [[strikeout]]s.
*[[1992]] &amp;ndash; [[Federal Bureau of Investigation|FBI]] [[Hostage Rescue Team|HRT]] [[sniper]] [[Lon Horiuchi]] shoots and kills Vicki Weaver during an 11-day siege at her home at [[Ruby Ridge]], [[Idaho]].
*[[1996]] &amp;ndash; [[Bill Clinton]] signs [[Personal Responsibility and Work Opportunity Act|welfare reform]] into law, representing major shift in US welfare policy
*[[2003]] &amp;ndash; [[Alabama]] [[Chief Justice]] [[Roy Moore]] is suspended after refusing to comply with a federal court order to remove a rock inscribed with the [[Ten Commandments]] from the lobby of the [[Alabama Supreme Court]] building.
*[[2004]] &amp;ndash; Versions of ''[[The Scream]]'' and ''[[Madonna (Edvard Munch)|Madonna]]'', two paintings by [[Edvard Munch]], are stolen at gunpoint from a museum in [[Oslo]], [[Norway]].
*[[2006]] &amp;ndash; [[Pulkovo Aviation Enterprise Flight 612]] crashes near the Russian border over eastern [[Ukraine]], killing all 170 people on board.
*[[2007]] &amp;ndash; The [[Texas Rangers (baseball)|Texas Rangers]] rout the [[Baltimore Orioles]] 30–3, the most runs scored by a team in modern MLB history.
*  2007   &amp;ndash; The [[Storm botnet]], a botnet created by the [[Storm Worm]], sends out a record 57 million e-mails in one day
*[[2012]] &amp;ndash; [[2012 Tana River District clashes|Ethnic clashes]] over [[grazing rights]] for cattle in Kenya's [[Tana River District]] result in more than 52 deaths.
