==Events==
*  [[69]] &amp;ndash; After the First [[Battle of Bedriacum]], [[Vitellius]] becomes [[Roman Emperor]].
*[[1080]] &amp;ndash; The [[List of Danish monarchs|King of Denmark]] [[Harald III of Denmark|Harald III]] dies and is succeeded by [[Canute IV of Denmark|Canute IV]], who would later be the first [[Danes|Dane]] to be [[canonization|canonized]].
*[[1349]] &amp;ndash; Fall of the [[Bavand dynasty]], and rise of the [[Afrasiyab dynasty]].
*[[1397]] &amp;ndash; [[Geoffrey Chaucer]] tells ''[[The Canterbury Tales]]'' for the first time at the court of [[Richard II of England|Richard II]]. Chaucer scholars have also identified this date (in 1387) as the start of the book's [[pilgrimage]] to [[Canterbury]].
*[[1492]] &amp;ndash; [[Spain]] and [[Christopher Columbus]] sign the [[Capitulations of Santa Fe]] for his voyage to [[Asia]] to acquire [[spice]]s.
*[[1521]] &amp;ndash; Trial of [[Martin Luther]] over his [[Lutheranism|teachings]] begins during the assembly of the [[Diet of Worms]]. Initially intimidated, he asks for time to reflect before answering and is given a stay of one day.
*[[1524]] &amp;ndash; [[Giovanni da Verrazzano]] reaches [[New York City|New York]] harbor.
*[[1555]] &amp;ndash; After 18 months of siege, [[Siena]] surrenders to the [[Florence|Florentine]]-[[Holy Roman Empire|Imperial]] army. The [[Republic of Siena]] is incorporated into the [[Grand Duchy of Tuscany]].
*[[1797]] &amp;ndash; Sir [[Ralph Abercromby]] [[Battle of San Juan (1797)|attacks]] [[San Juan, Puerto Rico]], in what would be one of the largest invasions of the Spanish territories in America.
*  1797   &amp;ndash; Citizens of [[Verona]], [[Italy]], begin an [[Veronese Easters|eight-day rebellion]] against the [[First French Republic|French]] occupying forces, which will end unsuccessfully.
*[[1863]] &amp;ndash; [[American Civil War]]: [[Grierson's Raid]] begins &amp;ndash; troops under [[Union Army]] Colonel [[Benjamin Grierson]] attack central [[Mississippi]].
*[[1864]] &amp;ndash; American Civil War: The [[Battle of Plymouth (1864)|Battle of Plymouth]] begins – [[Confederate States of America|Confederate]] forces attack [[Plymouth, North Carolina]].
*[[1895]] &amp;ndash; The [[Treaty of Shimonoseki]] between [[China]] and [[Japan]] is signed. This marks the end of the [[First Sino-Japanese War]], and the defeated [[Qing Empire]] is forced to renounce its claims on [[Korea]] and to concede the southern portion of the [[Liaoning|Fengtien]] [[History of the administrative divisions of China|province]], [[Taiwan]] and the [[Pescadores Islands]] to [[Japan]].
*[[1897]] &amp;ndash; The [[Aurora, Texas UFO incident]]
*[[1905]] &amp;ndash; The [[Supreme Court of the United States]] decides ''[[Lochner v. New York]]'', which holds that the &quot;[[Freedom of contract|right to free contract]]&quot; is implicit in the [[Due process|due process clause]] of the [[Fourteenth Amendment of the United States Constitution]].
*[[1907]] &amp;ndash; The [[Ellis Island]] immigration center processes 11,747 people, more than on any other day.
*[[1912]] &amp;ndash; [[Russian Empire|Russian]] troops [[Lena massacre|open fire]] on striking goldfield workers in northeast [[Siberia]], killing at least 150.
*[[1937]] &amp;ndash; [[Daffy Duck]]'s first appearance, in Porky's Duck Hunt.
*[[1941]] &amp;ndash; [[World War II]]: The [[Kingdom of Yugoslavia]] surrenders to [[Nazi Germany|Germany]].
*[[1942]] &amp;ndash; French [[prisoner of war]] General [[Henri Giraud]] escapes from his castle prison in [[Festung Königstein]].
*[[1944]] &amp;ndash; Forces of the Communist-controlled [[Greek People's Liberation Army]] attack the smaller [[National and Social Liberation]] resistance group, which surrenders. Its leader [[Dimitrios Psarros]] is murdered.
*[[1945]] &amp;ndash; [[World War II]]: Brazilian forces liberate the town of [[Montese]], Italy, from [[Nazi]] forces.
*[[1946]] &amp;ndash; Syria obtains its independence from the French occupation.
*[[1949]] &amp;ndash; At midnight 26 Irish counties officially leave the [[British Commonwealth]]. A 21-gun salute on O'Connell Bridge, [[Dublin]], ushers in the [[Republic of Ireland]].
*[[1951]] &amp;ndash; The [[Peak District]] becomes the United Kingdom's first [[National parks of England and Wales|National Park]].
*[[1961]] &amp;ndash; [[Bay of Pigs Invasion]]: A group of [[Cuban exile]]s financed and trained by the [[Central Intelligence Agency|CIA]] lands at the [[Bay of Pigs]] in Cuba with the aim of ousting [[Fidel Castro]].
*[[1964]] &amp;ndash; [[Jerrie Mock]] becomes the first woman to circumnavigate the world by air.
*  1964   &amp;ndash; [[Ford Mustang]] is introduced to the North American market.
*[[1969]] &amp;ndash; [[Sirhan Sirhan]] is convicted of assassinating [[Robert F. Kennedy]].
*  1969   &amp;ndash; [[Czechoslovakia]]n [[Communist Party]] chairman [[Alexander Dubček]] is deposed.
*[[1970]] &amp;ndash; [[Apollo program]]: The ill-fated [[Apollo 13]] spacecraft returns to [[Earth]] safely.
*[[1971]] &amp;ndash; The People's Republic of [[Bangladesh]] forms, under [[Sheikh Mujibur Rahman]] at Mujibnagor.
*[[1973]] &amp;ndash; [[George Lucas]] begins writing the treatment for [[Star Wars|The Star Wars]].
*[[1975]] &amp;ndash; The [[Cambodian Civil War]] ends. The [[Khmer Rouge]] captures the capital [[Phnom Penh]] and [[Khmer National Armed Forces|Cambodian government forces]] surrender.
*[[1978]] &amp;ndash; [[Mir Akbar Khyber]] is assassinated, provoking a [[Saur Revolution|communist coup d'état]] in [[Afghanistan]].
*[[1982]] &amp;ndash; Patriation of the [[Canadian constitution]] in [[Ottawa]] by [[Proclamation]] of [[Elizabeth II of the United Kingdom|Queen Elizabeth II]], [[Queen of Canada]].
*[[1984]] &amp;ndash; Police [[Constable]] [[Yvonne Fletcher]] is killed by gunfire from the [[History of Libya under Muammar Gaddafi#Great Socialist People's Libyan Arab Jamahiriya (1977–2012)|Libyan People's Bureau]] (Embassy) in [[London]] during a small demonstration outside the embassy. Ten others are wounded. The events lead to an 11-day [[siege]] of the building.
*[[1986]] &amp;ndash; The [[Three Hundred and Thirty Five Years' War]] between the [[Netherlands]] and the [[Isles of Scilly]] ends.
*  1986   &amp;ndash; [[Nezar Hindawi]]'s attempt to [[Hindawi affair|detonate a bomb]] aboard an [[El Al]] flight from [[London]] to [[Tel Aviv]] is thwarted.

*[[2006]] &amp;ndash; [[Sami Hammad]], a [[Palestinian people|Palestinian]] suicide bomber, detonates an explosive device in [[Tel Aviv]], killing 11 people and injuring 70.
*[[2013]] &amp;ndash; [[West Fertilizer Company explosion|An explosion]] at a fertilizer plant in the city of [[West, Texas]], kills 15 people and injures 160 others.
*[[2014]] &amp;ndash; [[NASA]]'s [[Kepler Telescope|Kepler]] confirms the discovery of [[Kepler-186f|the first Earth-size planet]] in the [[Circumstellar habitable zone|habitable zone]] of another star.
