==Events==
*[[215 BC]] &amp;ndash; [[Temple of Venus Erycina|A temple]] is built on the [[Capitoline Hill]] dedicated to [[Venus Erycina]] to commemorate the [[Roman Empire|Roman]] defeat at [[Battle of Lake Trasimene|Lake Trasimene]].
* [[599]] &amp;ndash; Maya king [[Scroll Serpent|Uneh Chan]] of [[Calakmul]] attacks rival city-state [[Palenque]] in southern Mexico, defeating queen [[Yohl Ik'nal]] and sacking the city.
* [[711]] &amp;ndash; [[Dagobert III]] is crowned [[King of the Franks]].
*[[1014]] &amp;ndash; [[Battle of Clontarf]]: [[Brian Boru]] defeats [[Viking]] invaders, but is killed in battle.
*[[1016]] &amp;ndash; [[Edmund Ironside]] succeeds his father [[Æthelred the Unready]] as [[king of England]].
*[[1343]] &amp;ndash; [[St. George's Night Uprising]] commences in the [[Danish Estonia#Duchy of Estonia|Duchy of Estonia]].
*[[1348]] &amp;ndash; The founding of the [[Order of the Garter]] by King [[Edward III of England|Edward III]] is announced on [[Saint George|St. George's Day]].
*[[1516]] &amp;ndash; The Bayerische [[Reinheitsgebot]] (regarding the ingredients of beer) is signed in [[Ingolstadt]].
*[[1521]] &amp;ndash; [[Battle of Villalar]]: [[Charles V, Holy Roman Emperor|King Charles I of Spain]] defeats the [[Revolt of the Comuneros|Comuneros]].
*[[1635]] &amp;ndash; The first public school in the United States, [[Boston Latin School]], is founded in [[Boston]].
*[[1655]] &amp;ndash; The [[Siege of Santo Domingo (1655)|Siege of Santo Domingo]] begins during the [[Anglo-Spanish War (1654–60)|Anglo-Spanish War]], and fails seven days later.
*[[1660]] &amp;ndash; [[Treaty of Oliwa]] is established between [[Sweden]] and [[Poland]].
*[[1661]] &amp;ndash; King [[Charles II of England]], [[Scotland]] and [[Ireland]] is crowned in [[Westminster Abbey]].
*[[1815]] &amp;ndash; [[Second Serbian Uprising|The Second Serbian Uprising]]: A second phase of the national revolution of the Serbs against the [[Ottoman Empire]], erupts shortly after the annexation of the country to the Ottoman Empire.
*[[1910]] &amp;ndash; [[President of the United States|American President]] [[Theodore Roosevelt]] makes his &quot;[[The Man in the Arena]]&quot; speech.
*[[1914]] &amp;ndash; First [[baseball]] game at [[History of Wrigley Field|Wrigley Field]], then known as Weeghman Park in Chicago. 
*[[1918]] &amp;ndash; [[World War I]]: The [[Royal Navy|British Royal Navy]] makes a [[Zeebrugge Raid|raid]] in an attempt to neutralise the [[Belgium|Belgian]] [[port of Bruges-Zeebrugge]].
*[[1920]] &amp;ndash; The [[Grand National Assembly of Turkey]] (TBMM) is founded in [[Ankara]], [[Turkey]]. It denounces the government of [[Sultan]] [[Mehmed VI]] and announces the preparation of a [[Turkish Constitution of 1921|temporary constitution]].
*[[1927]]   &amp;ndash; [[Cardiff City]] defeat [[Arsenal F.C.|Arsenal]] in the [[1927 FA Cup Final|FA Cup Final]], the only time it has been won by a team not based in England.
*[[1929]] &amp;ndash; [[Turkey]] becomes the first country to celebrate [[Children's Day]] as a national holiday.&lt;ref&gt;http://www.tbmm.gov.tr/kultursanat/23_Nisan.htm&lt;/ref&gt;
*[[1932]] &amp;ndash; The 153-year-old [[De Adriaan, Haarlem|De Adriaan]] Windmill in [[Haarlem]], [[Netherlands]] burns down. It is rebuilt and reopens exactly 70 years later.
*[[1935]] &amp;ndash; The [[Polish Constitution of 1935]] is adopted.
*[[1940]] &amp;ndash; The [[Rhythm Night Club fire]] at a dance hall in [[Natchez, Mississippi]], kills 198 people.
*[[1941]] &amp;ndash; [[World War II]]: The Greek government and [[George II of Greece|King George II]] evacuate [[Athens]] before the invading [[Wehrmacht]].
*[[1942]] &amp;ndash; World War II: [[Baedeker Blitz]] &amp;ndash; [[Germany|German]] bombers hit [[Exeter]], [[Bath, Somerset|Bath]] and [[York]] in retaliation for the British raid on [[Lübeck]].
*[[1945]] &amp;ndash; World War II: [[Adolf Hitler]]'s designated successor [[Hermann Göring]] sends him [[Göring Telegram|a telegram]] asking permission to take leadership of the [[Third Reich]], which causes Hitler to replace him with [[Joseph Goebbels]] and [[Karl Dönitz]].
*[[1946]] &amp;ndash; [[Manuel Roxas]] is elected the last President of the [[Commonwealth of the Philippines]].
*[[1949]] &amp;ndash; [[Chinese Civil War]]: Establishment of the [[People's Liberation Army Navy]].
*[[1951]] &amp;ndash; American journalist [[William N. Oatis]] is arrested for espionage by the [[Communist]] government of [[Czechoslovakia]].
*[[1955]] &amp;ndash; The [[Canadian Labour Congress]] is formed by the merger of the [[Trades and Labour Congress of Canada]] and the [[Canadian Congress of Labour]].
*[[1961]] &amp;ndash; [[Algiers putsch]] by French generals.
*[[1967]] &amp;ndash; [[Soviet space program]]: [[Soyuz 1]] (Russian: Союз 1, Union 1) a manned spaceflight carrying [[cosmonaut]] Colonel [[Vladimir Komarov]] is launched into orbit.
*[[1968]] &amp;ndash; [[Vietnam War]]: Student protesters at [[Columbia University]] in [[New York City]] [[Columbia University protests of 1968|take over administration buildings and shut down the university]].
*[[1971]] &amp;ndash; [[Bangladesh Liberation War]]: The [[Pakistan Army]] and [[Razakars (Pakistan)|Razakars]] [[Jathibhanga massacre|massacre]] approximately 3,000 [[Hinduism|Hindu]] emigrants in the Jathibhanga area of [[East Pakistan]] (now [[Bangladesh]]).
*[[1985]] &amp;ndash; [[Coca-Cola]] changes its formula and releases [[New Coke]]. The response is overwhelmingly negative, and the original formula is back on the market in less than three months.
*[[1990]] &amp;ndash; [[Namibia]] becomes the 160th member of the [[United Nations]] and the 50th member of the [[Commonwealth of Nations]].
*[[1993]] &amp;ndash; [[Eritrea]]ns vote overwhelmingly for independence from [[Ethiopia]] in a [[United Nations]]-monitored referendum.
*  1993   &amp;ndash; [[Sri Lanka]]n politician [[Lalith Athulathmudali]] is [[Assassination of Lalith Athulathmudali|assassinated]] while addressing a gathering, approximately four weeks ahead of the Provincial Council elections for the [[Western Province, Sri Lanka|Western Province]].
*[[1997]] &amp;ndash; [[Omaria massacre]] in [[Algeria]]: Forty-two villagers are killed.
*[[2005]] &amp;ndash; First [[Youtube]] video uploaded, titled &quot;Me at the zoo&quot;.
*[[2013]] &amp;ndash; [[April 2013 Bachu unrest|Violence]] in [[Bachu County]], [[Kashgar Prefecture]], of [[China]]'s [[Xinjiang]] results in death of 21 people.
