==Events==
*[[46 BC]] &amp;ndash; [[Julius Caesar]] defeats Caecilius Metellus Scipio and [[Cato the Younger|Marcus Porcius Cato (Cato the Younger)]] in the [[battle of Thapsus]].
* [[402]] &amp;ndash; [[Stilicho]] stymies the [[Visigoths]] under [[Alaric I|Alaric]] in the [[Battle of Pollentia]].
*[[1199]] &amp;ndash; [[King Richard I]] of England dies from an infection following the removal of an arrow from his shoulder.
*[[1250]] &amp;ndash; [[Seventh Crusade]]: [[Ayyubid dynasty|Ayyubids]] of Egypt capture King [[Louis IX of France]] in the [[Battle of Fariskur]].
*[[1320]] &amp;ndash; The [[Scotland|Scots]] reaffirm their independence by signing the [[Declaration of Arbroath]].
*[[1327]] &amp;ndash; The [[poet]] [[Petrarch]] first sees his idealized love, Laura, in the church of [[Saint Clare of Assisi|Saint Clare]] in [[Avignon]].
*[[1385]] &amp;ndash; John, Master of the [[Order of Aviz]], is made king [[John I of Portugal]].
*[[1453]] &amp;ndash; [[Mehmed II]] begins his siege of [[Constantinople]] ([[Istanbul]]), which falls on [[May 29]].
*[[1580]] &amp;ndash; One of the [[1580 Dover Straits earthquake|largest earthquakes]] recorded in the history of England, Flanders, or Northern France, takes place.
*[[1652]] &amp;ndash; At the [[Cape of Good Hope]], [[Netherlands|Dutch]] sailor [[Jan van Riebeeck]] establishes a resupply camp that eventually becomes [[Cape Town]].
*[[1667]] &amp;ndash; An [[earthquake]] devastates [[Dubrovnik]], then an independent [[city-state]].
*[[1712]] &amp;ndash; The [[New York Slave Revolt of 1712]] begins near [[Broadway (Manhattan)|Broadway]].
*[[1776]] &amp;ndash; [[American Revolutionary War]]: Ships of the [[Continental Navy]] fail in their [[Battle of Block Island|attempt to capture]] a [[Royal Navy]] [[dispatch boat]].
*[[1782]] &amp;ndash; King [[Buddha Yodfa Chulaloke]] (Rama I) of Siam (modern day [[Thailand]]) founded the Chakri dynasty.
*[[1793]] &amp;ndash; During the [[French Revolution]], the [[Committee of Public Safety]] becomes the executive organ of the republic.
*[[1808]] &amp;ndash; [[John Jacob Astor]] incorporates the [[American Fur Company]], that would eventually make him America's first millionaire.
*[[1812]] &amp;ndash; British forces under the command of the [[Duke of Wellington]] assault the fortress of [[Siege of Badajoz (1812)|Badajoz]]. This would be the turning point in the [[Peninsular War]] against [[Napoleon]]-led [[France]].
*[[1814]] &amp;ndash; Nominal beginning of the [[Bourbon Restoration]]; anniversary date that Napoleon abdicates and is exiled to [[Elba]].
*[[1830]] &amp;ndash; [[Church of Christ (Latter Day Saints)|Church of Christ]], the original church of the [[Latter Day Saint movement]], is organized by [[Joseph Smith, Jr.]] and others at [[Fayette, New York|Fayette]] or [[Manchester (town), New York|Manchester, New York]].
*[[1860]] &amp;ndash; The [[Reorganized Church of Jesus Christ of Latter Day Saints]]—later renamed [[Community of Christ]]—is organized by [[Joseph Smith III]] and others at [[Amboy, Illinois]]
*[[1861]] &amp;ndash; First performance of [[Arthur Sullivan]]'s debut success, his suite of [[incidental music]] for ''[[The Tempest (Sullivan)|The Tempest]]'', leading to a career that included the famous [[Gilbert and Sullivan]] operas.
*[[1862]] &amp;ndash; [[American Civil War]]: The [[Battle of Shiloh]] begins &amp;ndash; in [[Tennessee]], forces under [[Union (American Civil War)|Union]] [[General (United States)|General]] [[Ulysses S. Grant]] meet [[Confederate States of America|Confederate]] troops led by General [[Albert Sidney Johnston]].
*[[1865]] &amp;ndash; American Civil War: The [[Battle of Sayler's Creek]] &amp;ndash; Confederate General [[Robert E. Lee]]'s [[Army of Northern Virginia]] fights its last major battle while in retreat from [[Richmond, Virginia]].
*[[1866]] &amp;ndash; The [[Grand Army of the Republic]], an American patriotic organization composed of Union veterans of the American Civil War, is founded. It lasts until [[1956]].
*[[1869]] &amp;ndash; [[Celluloid]] is patented.
*[[1888]] &amp;ndash; [[Thomas Green Clemson]] dies, bequeathing his estate to the State of [[South Carolina]] to establish [[Clemson University|Clemson Agricultural College]].
*[[1893]] &amp;ndash; [[Salt Lake Temple]] of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints is dedicated by [[Wilford Woodruff]].
*[[1895]] &amp;ndash; [[Oscar Wilde]] is arrested in the [[Cadogan Hotel]], [[London]] after losing a [[libel]] case against the [[John Sholto Douglas, 9th Marquess of Queensberry|Marquess of Queensberry]].
*[[1896]] &amp;ndash; In [[Athens]], the opening of the [[1896 Summer Olympics|first modern Olympic Games]] is celebrated, 1,500 years after the original games are banned by [[Roman Emperor]] [[Theodosius I]].
*[[1909]] &amp;ndash; [[Robert Edwin Peary|Robert Peary]] and [[Matthew Henson]] reach the [[North Pole]].
*[[1911]] &amp;ndash; During the [[Battle of Deçiq]], [[Ded Gjo Luli|Dedë Gjon Luli Dedvukaj]], leader of the [[Malësori]] [[Albania]]ns, raises the Albanian flag in the town of [[Tuzi]], [[Montenegro]], for the first time after [[George Kastrioti]] ([[Skenderbeg]]).
*[[1917]] &amp;ndash; [[World War I]]: The United States declares war on [[Germany]] (see [[:s:Woodrow Wilson declares war on Germany|President Woodrow Wilson's address to Congress]]).
*[[1919]] &amp;ndash; [[Mohandas Karamchand Gandhi]] orders a [[general strike]].
*[[1923]] &amp;ndash; The first Prefects Board in [[Southeast Asia]] is formed in [[Victoria Institution]], [[Malaysia]].
*[[1924]] &amp;ndash; [[First aerial circumnavigation|First round-the-world]] flight commences.
*[[1926]] &amp;ndash; [[Varney Airlines]] makes its first commercial flight (Varney is the root company of [[United Airlines]]).
*[[1929]] &amp;ndash; [[Huey P. Long]] [[Governor of Louisiana]] is [[impeach]]ed by the [[Louisiana House of Representatives]].
*[[1930]] &amp;ndash; Gandhi raises a lump of mud and salt and declares, &quot;With this, I am shaking the foundations of the [[British Empire]],&quot; beginning the [[Salt Satyagraha]].
*[[1936]] &amp;ndash; [[Tupelo-Gainesville tornado outbreak]]: Another tornado from the same storm system as the Tupelo tornado hits [[Gainesville, Georgia]], killing 203.
*[[1941]] &amp;ndash; [[World War II]]: [[Nazi Germany]] launches [[Invasion of Yugoslavia|Operation 25]] (the invasion of Kingdom of Yugoslavia) and [[Operation Marita]] (the invasion of [[Greece]]).
*[[1945]] &amp;ndash; World War II: [[Sarajevo]] is liberated from [[Germany|German]] and [[Croatia]]n forces by the [[Yugoslav Partisans]].
*  1945   &amp;ndash; World War II: the [[Battle of Slater's Knoll]] on Bougainville comes to an end.
*[[1947]] &amp;ndash; The first [[Tony Awards]] are presented for theatrical achievement.
*[[1957]] &amp;ndash; Greek shipping tycoon [[Aristotle Onassis]] buys the Hellenic National Airlines (TAE) and founds [[Olympic Airlines]].
*[[1962]] &amp;ndash; [[Leonard Bernstein]] causes controversy with his remarks from the podium during a [[New York Philharmonic]] [[New York Philharmonic concert of April 6, 1962|concert]] featuring [[Glenn Gould]] performing [[Johannes Brahms|Brahms]]' [[Piano Concerto No. 1 (Brahms)|First Piano Concerto]].
*[[1965]] &amp;ndash; Launch of [[Intelsat I|Early Bird]], the first communications [[satellite]] to be placed in [[geosynchronous orbit]].
*  1965   &amp;ndash; The British Government announces the cancellation of the [[BAC TSR-2|TSR-2]] aircraft project.
*[[1968]] &amp;ndash; In [[Richmond, Indiana]]'s downtown district, a [[Richmond, Indiana explosion|double explosion]] kills 41 and injures 150.
*  1968   &amp;ndash; [[Pierre Elliot Trudeau]] wins the [[Liberal Party of Canada leadership election, 1968|Liberal Leadership Election]], and becomes Prime Minister of [[Canada]] soon after.
*[[1970]] &amp;ndash; [[Newhall Incident]]: Four [[California Highway Patrol]] officers are killed in a shootout.
*[[1972]] &amp;ndash; [[Vietnam War]]: [[Easter Offensive]] &amp;ndash; American forces begin sustained air strikes and naval bombardments.
*[[1973]] &amp;ndash; Launch of ''[[Pioneer 11]]'' [[spacecraft]].
*  1973   &amp;ndash; The [[American League]] of [[Major League Baseball]] begins using the [[designated hitter]].
*[[1974]] &amp;ndash; The Swedish pop band [[ABBA]] wins the [[Eurovision Song Contest 1974]] with the song &quot;[[Waterloo (ABBA song)|Waterloo]]&quot;, launching their international career.
*[[1982]] &amp;ndash; [[Estonian Communist Party]] bureau declares &quot;fight against bourgeois TV&quot;—meaning [[Finland|Finnish]] TV—a top priority of the propagandists of [[Estonian SSR]]
*[[1984]] &amp;ndash; Members of [[Cameroon]]'s Republican Guard unsuccessfully attempt to overthrow the [[government]] headed by [[Paul Biya]].
*[[1994]] &amp;ndash; The [[Rwandan Genocide]] begins when the aircraft carrying [[Rwanda]]n [[president]] [[Juvénal Habyarimana]] and [[Burundi]]an president [[Cyprien Ntaryamira]] is shot down.
*[[1998]] &amp;ndash; [[Pakistan]] tests medium-range missiles capable of reaching [[India]].
*  1998   &amp;ndash; [[Travelers Group]] announces an agreement to undertake the $76 billion merger between Travelers and [[Citicorp]], and the merger is completed on [[October 8]], of that year, forming [[Citibank]].
*[[2004]] &amp;ndash; [[Rolandas Paksas]] becomes the first president of [[Lithuania]] to be peacefully removed from office by [[impeachment]].
*[[2005]] &amp;ndash; Kurdish leader [[Jalal Talabani]] becomes [[Iraq]]i president; Shiite Arab Ibrahim al-Jaafari is named [[premier]] the next day.
*[[2008]] &amp;ndash; [[2008 Egyptian general strike]] starts led by [[Egypt]]ian workers later to be adopted by [[April 6 Youth Movement]] and [[Egypt]]ian activities .
*[[2009]] &amp;ndash; A [[2009 L'Aquila earthquake|6.3 magnitude earthquake]] strikes near [[L'Aquila]], [[Italy]], killing 307.
*[[2010]] &amp;ndash; [[Communist Party of India (Maoist)|Maoist rebels]] [[April 2010 Maoist attack in Dantewada|kill]] 76 [[Central Reserve Police Force|CRPF]] officers in [[Dantewada district]], [[India]].
*[[2011]] &amp;ndash; In [[San Fernando, Tamaulipas]], [[Mexico]], over 193 bodies were exhumed from several [[2011 San Fernando massacre|mass graves]] made by [[Los Zetas]].
*[[2012]] &amp;ndash; [[Azawad]] [[Azawadi declaration of independence|declares itself independent]] from the [[Republic of Mali]].
