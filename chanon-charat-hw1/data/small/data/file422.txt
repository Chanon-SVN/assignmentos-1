American Samoa has a total of 241&amp;nbsp;km of [[highway]]s (estimated in 2008).&lt;ref name=&quot;CIA&quot;&gt;[https://www.cia.gov/library/publications/the-world-factbook/geos/aq.html &quot;American Samoa.&quot;] [https://www.cia.gov/library/publications/the-world-factbook CIA - The World Factbook]. Accessed June 2011.&lt;/ref&gt; Ports and [[harbor]]s include [[Aunuu|Aunu‘u]], [[Auasi]], [[Tau, Samoa|Faleāsao]], [[Ofu-Olosega#Ofu|Ofu]] and [[Pago Pago]].&lt;ref name=&quot;CIA&quot;/&gt; American Samoa has no railways.&lt;ref name=&quot;CIA&quot;/&gt; The territory has three [[airport]]s, all of which have paved runways. The main airport is [[Pago Pago International Airport]].&lt;ref name=&quot;CIA&quot;/&gt; Per a 1999 estimate, there are no [[merchant marine]]s in American Samoa.&lt;ref name=&quot;CIA&quot;/&gt;
