==Events==
*[[1056]] &amp;ndash; After a sudden illness a few days previously, [[List of Byzantine emperors|Byzantine Empress]] [[Theodora (11th century)|Theodora]] dies childless, thus ending the [[Macedonian dynasty]].
*[[1218]] &amp;ndash; [[Al-Kamil]] becomes [[Sultan]] of Egypt, Syria and northern Mesopotamia on the death of his father [[Al-Adil]].
*[[1314]] &amp;ndash; King [[Håkon V of Norway|Håkon V Magnusson]] moves the capital of [[Norway]] from [[Bergen]] to [[Oslo]].
*[[1422]] &amp;ndash; King [[Henry V of England]] dies of [[dysentery]] while in [[France]]. His son, [[Henry VI of England|Henry VI]] becomes King of England at the age of 9 months.
*[[1795]] &amp;ndash; [[War of the First Coalition]]: The British [[Capture of Trincomalee (1795)|capture Trincomalee]] (present-day [[Sri Lanka]]) from the Dutch in order to keep it out of French hands.
*[[1798]] &amp;ndash; [[Irish Rebellion of 1798]]: Irish rebels, with French assistance, establish the short-lived [[Republic of Connacht]].
*[[1803]] &amp;ndash; [[Lewis and Clark Expedition|Lewis and Clark]] start their expedition to the west by leaving  [[Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania]] at 11 in the morning.
*[[1813]] &amp;ndash; At the final stage of the [[Peninsular War]], British-Portuguese troops capture the town of Donostia (now [[San Sebastián]]), resulting in a [[Siege of San Sebastián|rampage and eventual destruction]] of the town. Elsewhere, Spanish troops repel a French attack in the [[Battle of San Marcial]].
*[[1864]] &amp;ndash; During the [[American Civil War]], [[United States|Union]] forces led by General [[William T. Sherman]] launch an [[Battle of Jonesborough|assault]] on [[Atlanta, Georgia]].
*[[1876]] &amp;ndash; [[Ottoman Sultan]] [[Murat V]] is deposed and succeeded by his brother [[Abd-ul-Hamid II]].
*[[1886]] &amp;ndash; An [[Charleston earthquake|earthquake]] kills 100 in [[Charleston, South Carolina]].
*[[1888]] &amp;ndash; [[Mary Ann Nichols]] is murdered. She is the first of [[Jack the Ripper]]'s confirmed victims.
*[[1895]] &amp;ndash; German Count [[Ferdinand von Zeppelin]] patents his ''[[Zeppelin|Navigable Balloon]]''.
*[[1897]] &amp;ndash; [[Thomas Edison]] patents the [[Kinetoscope]], the first movie projector.
*[[1907]] &amp;ndash; Count [[Alexander Izvolsky]] and Sir [[Arthur Nicolson, 1st Baron Carnock|Arthur Nicolson]] sign the [[Anglo-Russian Entente|St. Petersburg Convention]], which results in the [[Triple Entente]] [[military alliance|alliance]].
*[[1913]] &amp;ndash; Police attack a union rally during the [[Dublin Lock-out]], killing two people and injuring hundreds.
*[[1918]] &amp;ndash; [[World War I]]: Start of the [[Battle of Mont Saint-Quentin]], a successful assault by the [[Australian Corps]] during the [[Hundred Days Offensive]].
*[[1920]] &amp;ndash; [[Polish-Bolshevik War]]: A decisive [[Poland|Polish]] victory in the [[Battle of Komarów]].
*  1920   &amp;ndash; The first [[radio]] news program is broadcast by [[WWJ (AM)|8MK]] in [[Detroit, Michigan]].
*[[1936]] &amp;ndash; [[Radio Prague]], now the official [[international broadcasting]] station of the Czech Republic, goes on the air.
*[[1935]] &amp;ndash; In an attempt to stay out of the growing turmoil in Europe, the United States passes the first of its [[Neutrality Acts of 1930s|Neutrality Acts]].
*[[1939]] &amp;ndash; [[Nazi Germany]] mounts a staged [[Gleiwitz incident|attack on the Gleiwitz radio station]], creating an excuse to attack [[Poland]] the following day thus starting [[European Theatre of World War II|World War II in Europe]].
*[[1940]] &amp;ndash; [[Lovettsville Air Disaster|Pennsylvania Central Airlines Trip 19]] crashes near [[Lovettsville, Virginia]]. The [[Civil Aeronautics Authority|CAB]] investigation of the accident is the first investigation to be conducted under the [[Bureau of Air Commerce]] act of 1938.
*[[1941]] &amp;ndash; [[World War II]]: [[Chetniks|Serbian paramilitary forces]] defeat Germans in the [[Battle of Loznica (1941)|Battle of Loznica]].
*[[1943]] &amp;ndash; The {{USS|Harmon|DE-678|6}}, the first [[United States Navy|U.S. Navy]] ship to be named after a black person, is commissioned.
*[[1945]] &amp;ndash; The [[Liberal Party of Australia]] is founded by [[Robert Menzies]].
*[[1949]] &amp;ndash; The retreat of the [[Democratic Army of Greece]] in [[Albania]] after its defeat on [[Gramos]] mountain marks the end of the [[Greek Civil War]].
*[[1957]] &amp;ndash; The [[Federation of Malaya]] (now [[Malaysia]]) gains its independence from the [[United Kingdom]].
*[[1958]] &amp;ndash; A [[parcel bomb]] sent by [[Ngo Dinh Nhu]], younger brother and chief adviser of [[South Vietnam]]ese President [[Ngo Dinh Diem]], fails to kill King [[Norodom Sihanouk]] of [[Cambodia]].
*[[1962]] &amp;ndash; [[Trinidad and Tobago]] becomes independent.
*[[1963]] &amp;ndash; [[North Borneo]] (now [[Sabah]]) achieve a [[self governance]].
*[[1965]] &amp;ndash; The [[Aero Spacelines Super Guppy]] aircraft makes its first flight.
*[[1968]] &amp;ndash; [[Garfield Sobers]] became the first batsman ever to hit six sixes in a single over of six consecutive balls in first-class cricket.
*[[1980]] &amp;ndash; After two weeks of nationwide [[strike action|strikes]], the Polish government was forced to sign the [[Gdańsk Agreement]], allowing for the creation of the trade union [[Solidarity (Polish trade union)|Solidarity]].
*  1980   &amp;ndash; [[Ogunpa River|Flood]] in [[Ibadan]] after 12 hours of heavy downpour killed over 300 people and properties worth million destroyed.  
*[[1982]] &amp;ndash; [[August 31, 1982 demonstrations in Poland|Anti-government demonstrations]] are held in 66 Polish cities to commemorate the second anniversary of the [[Gdańsk Agreement]].
*[[1986]] &amp;ndash; [[Aeroméxico Flight 498]] collides with a [[Piper Cherokee|Piper PA-28]] over [[Cerritos, California|Cerritos]], [[California]], killing 67 in the air and 15 on the ground.
*  1986   &amp;ndash; The [[Soviet Union|Soviet]] passenger liner {{Ship||Admiral Nakhimov|ship|6}} sinks in the [[Black Sea]] after colliding with the bulk carrier ''Pyotr Vasev'', killing 423.
*[[1987]] &amp;ndash; [[Thai Airways Flight 365]] crashes into the ocean near [[Phuket Province|Ko Phuket]], Thailand, killing all 83 aboard.
*[[1991]] &amp;ndash; [[Kyrgyzstan]] declares its independence from the [[Soviet Union]].
*[[1992]] &amp;ndash; [[Pascal Lissouba]] is inaugurated as the President of the [[Republic of the Congo]].
*[[1994]] &amp;ndash; The [[Provisional Irish Republican Army]] declares a [[ceasefire]].
*[[1996]] &amp;ndash; [[Saddam Hussein]]'s troops seized Irbil after the Kurdish [[Masoud Barzani]] appealed for help to defeat his Kurdish rival [[Patriotic Union of Kurdistan|PUK]].
*[[1997]] &amp;ndash; [[Diana, Princess of Wales]], her companion [[Dodi Fayed]] and driver [[Henri Paul]] [[Death of Diana, Princess of Wales|die in a car crash]] in [[Paris]].
*[[1998]] &amp;ndash; [[North Korea]] reportedly launches ''[[Kwangmyŏngsŏng-1]]'', its first [[artificial satellite|satellite]].
*[[1999]] &amp;ndash; The first of a series of [[Russian Apartment Bombings|bombings]] in [[Moscow]] kills one person and wounds 40 others.
*  1999   &amp;ndash; A [[Líneas Aéreas Privadas Argentinas|LAPA]] [[Boeing 737]]-200 [[LAPA Flight 3142|crashes during takeoff]] from [[Aeroparque Jorge Newbery|Jorge Newbury Airport]] in [[Buenos Aires]], killing 65, including two on the ground.
*[[2005]] &amp;ndash; A [[Baghdad bridge stampede|stampede]] on [[Al-Aaimmah bridge]] in [[Baghdad]] kills 1,199 people.
*[[2006]] &amp;ndash; [[Edvard Munch]]'s famous painting ''[[The Scream]]'', stolen on August 22, 2004, is recovered in a raid by [[Norway|Norwegian]] police.
